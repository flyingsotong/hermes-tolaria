1. **Lateral stability refers to the stability of an aircraft about which axis?**
   - **Longitudinal axis** ✓
   - Lateral axis
   - Vertical axis
   - Normal axis

2. **If an aircraft is disturbed from straight and level flight and it then regains its straight and level flight without pilot input, it is said to have:**
   - lots of static stability only
   - **positive dynamic stability** ✓
   - negative dynamic stability
   - none of these

3. **Directional stability and lateral stability is affected by which of the following factors?**
   - Vertical stabiliser
   - C of G
   - Wing sweepback and dihedral
   - Propeller position
   - **All of these** ✓

4. **Controllability and stability work against each other.**
   - **True** ✓
   - False

5. **If an aircraft is disturbed from normal flight and it then wants to stray further from normal flight, the aircraft is said to have negative static stability.**
   - False
   - **True** ✓

6. **Static instability is the same as negative static stability.**
   - **True** ✓
   - False

7. **Positive static stability is when an aircraft is displaced and it naturally returns to its pre-disturbed state.**
   - **True** ✓
   - False

8. **Lateral stability is affected by:**
   - **Wing dihedral and sweepback** ✓
   - Longitudinal dihedral and centre of gravity position
   - Wing position only
   - Directional stability and rudder trim

9. **Neutral static stability is the same as neutral stability.**
   - **True** ✓
   - False

10. **A wing with a sweptback leading edge has more lateral stability than a wing with a straight leading edge.**
    - **more** ✓
    - less
    - the same

11. **Which statement best describes positive static stability?**
    - The aircraft immediately returns to its original attitude after a disturbance
    - **The aircraft shows a tendency to return toward its original attitude after a disturbance** ✓
    - The aircraft shows no tendency to move toward or away from its original attitude after a disturbance
    - The aircraft moves further away from its original attitude after a disturbance

12. **A low wing is more laterally stable than a high wing.**
    - True
    - **False** ✓

13. **A very stable aircraft is also highly controllable.**
    - True
    - **False** ✓

14. **If an aircraft is disturbed from straight and level flight and it then fails to regain its straight and level flight without pilot input, it is said to have:**
    - no stability
    - positive static stability
    - **negative dynamic stability** ✓
    - neutral dynamic stability

15. **If an aircraft is disturbed from normal flight and the aircraft stays exactly in its disturbed state without correcting or getting worse, then it is said to have:**
    - positive static stability
    - negative static stability
    - **neutral static stability** ✓
    - none of these

16. **Longitudinal stability mainly affects pitch.**
    - **True** ✓
    - False

17. **Longitudinal stability is affected by which of the following factors?**
    - Power setting and Propeller position
    - C of G and C of P
    - Horizontal stabiliser
