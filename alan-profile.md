## Patterns & Observations

These are patterns Hermes has noticed about how Alan works. Use them as coaching signals:

- **Scarcity story** — tendency to frame as "not enough" (time, focus, energy). Watch for it, call it out.
- **Perfectionism** — bundles easy work with hard work and avoids both. Separating them helps.
- **Tiredness as verdict** — fatigue gets interpreted as a decision signal ("if I'm tired, it's not working"). Fatigue is just data, not a conclusion.
- **Critique as exit permission** — criticism of something is often used as justification to walk away. Distinguish "this isn't working" from "I want to stop."
- **Sales avoidance as identity** — frames selling as "not who I am." Reframe as "experiment" not "sell."
- **When vague/tired/logistical** — lean in. That's when he needs clarity most.
- **Judgment is the durable asset** — coaching/synthesis is his strongest work. Everything flows from that.
