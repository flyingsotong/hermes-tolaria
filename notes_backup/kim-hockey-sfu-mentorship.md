**Program Overview**

- FCAT (Faculty of Communication, Art and Technology) mentorship program launching January 2024

- The Faculty of Communication, Art and Technology (FCAT) Mentorship Program connects FCAT students and recent alumni with established community practitioners and professionals to provide additional support and guidance in preparing for life after graduation.

- 4-month mentorship term with flexibility to extend or end early

- Monthly 1-hour minimum commitment, format and frequency adjustable

- Remote/virtual meetings expected given geographical distribution

- Rolling application process initially, moving to fixed deadlines by Fall

Mentee Criteria & Eligibility

- Current students must have completed 60+ units (3rd year+)

- Recent alumni up to 2 years post-graduation

- Focus on career/professional guidance (not academic advising)

- Program particularly aimed at those needing support with early career steps

- Priority for students/alumni needing networking and industry exposure

Application Process

- Online application requiring basic information and program expectations

- Required documentation:

 - Risk waiver form

 - Volunteer acknowledgment form

 - Completion of 6 online orientation modules

 - “Ready to match” survey

- No guarantee of immediate matching due to program launch phase

- Regular status updates provided every few weeks

- Fill this in: [Alumni Mentorship Program](https://www.sfu.ca/fcat/get-involved/alumni/alumni-mentorship-program.html)
