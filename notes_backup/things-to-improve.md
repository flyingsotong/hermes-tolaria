**Forced landings**

- Wind

- Surroundings: Power lines, trees

- Size and shape: In relation to wind

- Surface and slope

- Civilization: Close proximity if possible


- learn radio calls for joining crosswind

- do not kill the power on landing — if the airplane is too high, it may crash it

- get comfortable with letting the plane glide above the ground... nose up and let it touch the ground

- focus on the turn to base — get that procedure perfect

1. End of downwind, set 1,800 rpm

2. Look out

3. Level turn 30 degrees of bank

4. White arc — flaps down

5. Radio call

6. Turn to final at 800 ft
