**What pilots and aircrafts fear: Hot, High, Humid!**

• Density is inversely proportional to temperature

**• Hot temperature = low air density\
**• Density is directly proportional to pressure — decreases with altitude\
• High altitude = low air density\
• Density of air reduces with increase in humidity\
• Humid environment = low air density\
• Low air density results in poor aircraft performance!\
• Robs away engine power\
• Reduces lift by the wing\
• Reduces propeller efficiency
