Thanks for booking a slot!

Here's a little more context to how I'm approaching coaching in the context of what we do at Splice.

My goal here is to help you form an awareness of your thinking around your media career or media entrepreneurship — what matters to you, what's causing you anxiety, and what you need to think through the next few steps of your journey.

Ultimately, I want to help you on your journey of change. Use me as a mirror and sounding board to understand your challenges.

My job is to help you understand what's holding you back so that you can remove that obstacle. The goal isn't for me to provide you with a solution or advice — that comes from within you. And if we're doing this right, you'll get a deeper understanding of yourself, what drives you, and what's holding you back.

The session is led by you — you pick something you want to work on. It could be something as simple as: "I'm afraid I'll fail." / "I'm worried that I won't find customers." / "I feel like I don't know enough to be doing this." / "I think my career has stalled."

And we'll take it from there.

Your trust matters to me. Our conversations are confidential. I may record our chat so I can improve as a coach — so let me know if you're not comfortable with that.

Let me know if this is in line with what you had in mind when you booked this. No worries if it isn't; it's ok to cancel.

You don't need to pay me for this (I am after all learning with you on this journey), but if you find these sessions useful, simply leave a donation in our Splice Spiffy Fund, which we use to help folks in the community with the opportunities, tools, and resources they need to grow: https://www.splicemedia.com/spiffy

Alternatively, you can make a donation of your choice to your favorite charity, temple, mosque, media startup, journalist, animal shelter or school. Or just buy someone a drink to let them know that they matter to you.

Should we set up our first 30-min online session to talk about what you'd like out of coaching and see if we can do something together?
