**Summary of Terms**
-**Sphere (SPH):** Corrects for nearsightedness (-) or farsightedness (+).
-**Cylinder (CYL):** Corrects for astigmatism.
-**Axis:** The orientation of the astigmatism correction.
-**NV-ADD:** The additional power for reading, used to correct presbyopia.
-**Pupillary Distance (PD):** The distance from your pupil to the center of your nose, ensuring your lenses are correctly aligned.
