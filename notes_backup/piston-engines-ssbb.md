**1. Suck**

The piston sucks a mixture of**air** from the intake manifold and atomized diesel**fuel** from an injector.

**2. Squeeze**

The piston squeezes the diesel and air into a very small volume, on the order of 400 psi, which also heats them to a very high temperature.

**3. Bang**

When the temperature exceeds the self-ignition temperature, the mixture explodes with a bang, driving the piston downward with the expanding gas and rotating the crank shaft.

**4. Blow**

The piston blows the exhaust products out the exhaust manifold and toward the muffler.\
