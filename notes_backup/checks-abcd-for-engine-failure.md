_In aviation, anything you do goes back to these 3 core principles: AVIATE, NAVIGATE and then COMMUNICATE._

_Always ensure that best glide speed is maintained so you have max range._

_Only proceed with C\&D when A\&B has been carried out and maintained!_

_This is a matter of LIFE and DEATH and should not to be taken lightly._
