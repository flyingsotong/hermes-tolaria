Feedback given to organizers of _Building Collective Alliance - Women Journalists and Human Rights Defenders Against Gendered Misinformation (Asia-Pacific Regional Convening - Bangkok, Thailand, 4-6 September 2023)_

**1. Obsess over what your participants want to get out of the event.** For some, it's a new awareness over an issue, or there's a specific problem they're trying to solve for themselves. For others, it is to meet specific people so they can build relationships with.

**2. Communicate clearly and consistently about the expected outcomes.**Publish the agenda on a website (password protected, if needed) so that people can plan ahead for this. Indicate clearly what outcomes are expected for your participants.

**3. Connect people before the event.**Publish a participant list (again, password protected if needed) so that people can form connections before they show up.

**4. Keep the doors open for relationships after the event.**Keep people connected on an email group, a WhatsApp group, etc. Help people stay connected.

**5. Push for a diversity in perspectives.**That means having more people from different experiences, nationalities, and industries.
