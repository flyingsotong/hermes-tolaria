**How AI will transform consumer technology <https://gamma.app/docs/a16z-Consumer-Abundance-Agenda-ieotbnzbxj81biu?mode=doc>**

1. Creativity + Productivity: Every consumer can be an author, programmer, designer, artist, poet, movie producer, musician, and more. Every employee, freelancer, and SMB owner can spend all their time on their craft instead of admin overhead.

2. Companionship + Social: Every consumer can have an infinitely empathetic, patient, interested, and engaging friend, lover, sparring partner. Every consumer can also help create and belong to a community tailored to their unique kind of weird.

3. Wellness + Personal Growth: Every consumer can have a team of teachers, doctors, therapists, and wealth managers to help them live their best life. Insights and support are delivered via a "push" model, arriving at the right time.
