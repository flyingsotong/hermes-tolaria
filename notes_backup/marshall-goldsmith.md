Want to improve at anything? Try Goldsmith's daily questions:

1\. Write 6 Qs (Did I do my best to be happy?)

2\. Score yourself 1-10 each day

3\. Get your report card each week

The scores will humble you. But awareness drives change over time.

• Get help. Don't let ego stop your growth.

• Surround yourself with those who lift you higher.

• Detach self-worth from achievements.

• You can shed your old skin. Change your identity to change your life.

Goldsmith's methods aren't complex. But the fundamentals, applied relentlessly, are transformative — in business and in life.

That's why the world's top CEOs pay him a fortune.
