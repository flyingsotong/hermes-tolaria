**November 27, 2023**

- It was a bit of a rambling call.
- She had just returned from Israel and was back to a regular routine. Her assumption is that by chasing the next news cycle, she would be in a position to create new opportunities for herself.
- But at the same time you get the sense that she isn't happy with the options in front of her and the bets she's making in the space. The assumption I kept calling out was that she really wanted the next big story, with the hope that that leads to something more permanent.
- She talked about wanting to move to Tel Aviv but at the same time knew that would not be a choice she would want for herself — that she would not have the personal life she wanted.
- The call was a bit of a rambling one. She has a lot of thinking to do around her assumption of what would make her happy and what wouldn't — and also what is actually within her control at a time when freelancing and the media landscape are changing as quickly as they are. I'm not sure if this was an incredibly helpful session for her because these are things she already knows, but maybe she just wanted to talk.
- In hindsight, I didn't ask the hard question (which says more about me than it does her): why does this matter to you? Why does it matter that you're chasing the next big story?
