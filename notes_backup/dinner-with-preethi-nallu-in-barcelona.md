Fri, March 15th, 2024

- 7:00pm met with [Preethi Nallu.md](Fri,%20March%2015th,%202024/Preethi%20Nallu.md) in Barcelona

 - I got to know her better, learning about her childhood and upbringing. Her hometown is Hyderabad, and she left there at 18 to attend university in the U.S. Afterward, she switched to an American passport. She's worked in numerous places around the world and was born in Iran, which is quite interesting. If I remember correctly, her father was a doctor. In her 20s, she spent a decade without speaking to her family, likely because she made choices to live in places they didn't approve of. We had a meaningful discussion about that. We also talked a lot about the industry. It turns out she knows many people I do as well. She's done considerable work for IMS and other NGOs in this field.
