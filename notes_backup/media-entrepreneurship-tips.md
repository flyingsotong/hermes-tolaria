If you're an early media startup, don't ask for money. Ask for buy-in for your mission and utility. Is what you're doing really important for your audience?

You're not starting from scratch. You're starting from experience. That experience manifests in how you think, feel, and act. That experience helps you reduce costs.

Chart your own path. But first understand why you're doing this and what makes you different. Set your own goals that are right for you.

Never assume that content is your product. It almost never is the solution.

Revenue matters. So do costs. Make sure your costs reflect your revenue opportunity.

It's ok to have two thoughts in your head. Don't waste all your time and money trying to find the right answer. It's ok to accept that often there's no right answer.
