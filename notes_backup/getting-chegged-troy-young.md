[Troy Young](Troy%20Young.md) <https://www.peoplevsalgorithms.com/p/getting-chegged>

Social media was just a prologue to a much longer and painful story of media disruption. It's AI that will truly confound long held notions of how media is made and distributed and the underlying economics that tie the whole thing together.

AI will certainly exacerbate trends that so many established media companies ignored for too long:

1. Attention is finite, content is virtually infinite, consumers learn to cope with this reality; that means you need to deliver something inherently unique. The world has gone from finite to infinite.

2. Consumers have little regard for "professional" conventions media companies regard as sacred;

3. YOU, the audience, are the center of this world;

4. Structural distribution advantages are fleeting on the network.
