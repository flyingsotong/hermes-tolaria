 - I had a good chat with [Nick Guan.md](Thu,%20January%2018th,%202024/Nick%20Guan.md) today.

 - Nick was introduced by Damien Tan and runs Amok TV, a small production house with about 10 people on his team. They're all video producers. The company's been around for 10 years, but the challenge now is how to keep things going as the landscape changes. He hasn't figured out how to make that change and take his team along with him. Interestingly, he has hired culture change coaches in the past, reflecting his understanding of his gaps and the team's needs.

 - Much of the conversation focused on the future of his company and his needs — I was impressed by his openness, his love for his team, and the responsibility that he feels in trying to help them figure out what's ahead in their careers. He wants to step back and let the business run itself while he works on other projects, which isn't surprising after 10 years of building it.
