- Audio memos

 - Recording this audio memo on my last day here in Siem Reap, Cambodia. This was the first trip I've done without any contacts. There was no job to be done, no conference to attend, and certainly no family event. This was probably the first time I've done something like this in about 10 years, and it's been quite a remarkable experience. I'm here for the Angkor Photo Festival, but I haven't attended many of their events. From what I've seen, the photography scene has changed since I was last here in 2007. The work hasn't been interesting to me — maybe I've changed — but the community has been okay. It's been a small turnout this year, and from my conversations with Jessica Lim, she is quite disappointed at the overall turnout. I think a large part has to do with the marketing. It's been very quiet and there hasn't been much promotion leading up to the event.

Anyway, I'm spending my time well; the afternoons have been great. I've been going for massages, taking walks, doing a bit of work. I brought the gaming PC (Lzmod + Ryzen 5600 + RTX 4060 LP) here just to see what was possible, and I've been spending the evenings at the festival projections. Apart from that, it's been really quiet. I've been hanging out with several Singaporean photographers, including a guy called Kevin Lee. It's been quiet and relaxed, and I'm really glad I did this for myself. This is what I've been craving for in 2023 — unfettered, unrushed moments of thought. I hope to do more of this this year, to get away from everything and not feel like I need to be working or at home all the time with the kids.
