Frequently Asked Questions What is the easiest way to learn Malay in Singapore? Learning the Malay language is fundamentally simple to learn as it is written in the Roman alphabet. The pronunciaton of words in the Malay language is also simple as the alphabets are pronounced just as it would be in English. Generally, the word order in Malay is similar to word order in an English sentence. Thus, this makes learning Malay very easy.

The easiest way to learn Malay is to...

1. Get a Malay Phrasebook and write simple phrases and words on Flashcards and memorize them

2. Study the Malay fundamentals, e.g. classifiers, how to construct questions, pronunciation etc.

3. Try and find a good Malay teacher or other person to talk with you or enroll in a Malay language class or visit Malaysia for more immersion
