Wed, July 3rd, 2024

- 1:00pm Docu+ with Danny Kim and Kevin Park

 - They were keen to figure out where the business goes and how it works. It comes down to three parts in this business. One, they are documentary filmmakers and are looking for a pathway toward doing something like that. The trouble is, even for a three-year-old business, the journey and the sales cycle for that is incredibly long. Second is the content production part of what they do. They create branded content, video format for marketing teams. Third, they are also dabbling in the creator space, creating very short videos that run on YouTube, for example.

 - We talked about where they see these opportunities, which is a weird conversation for me because I don't understand much about this space yet. I wanted them to understand that they are in a very crowded space with little distinction between what they're doing and what everyone else is doing. I told them of the need to focus on the work they do, firstly as a community, but also how they talk about their work and the importance of brand in this space. Branding is your profit margin, comparing yourself with everyone else out there.

 - I said he can reach out whenever he wants to. I didn't do a hard sell or anything. I don't think we're at that point yet, but let's see where this goes.
