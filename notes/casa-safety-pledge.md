# CASA Safety pledge

**My Safety Is in My Hands**
**My Commitment to Safety**
I understand that my safety — and the safety of others — is in my hands.
As a pilot, I recognise the privilege and responsibility that comes with flying.
To ensure the safety of myself, my passengers, and the broader aviation community,
I commit to upholding the highest standards of safety every time I fly.
** My Safety Actions1. Continuously Improve My Skills and Knowledge**
I will regularly engage in safety training and keep my pilot knowledge up to date,
knowing that ongoing learning is critical to my success as a safe pilot.
**2. Respect the Skies and My Peers**
I will be considerate of other pilots, air traffic control, and all aviation professionals,
fostering a safety-first culture in the aviation community.
**3. Speak Up for Safety**
I will actively promote safe practices and report unsafe conditions or behaviour,
knowing that we all share responsibility for the safety of our skies.
**4. Stay Alert and Focused**
I commit to flying only when I am physically and mentally fit,
avoiding fatigue or distractions that could compromise safety.
**5. Foster a Culture of Safety and Continuous Learning**
I will guide my peers, mentees, or students in developing safe flying habits,
critical decision-making, and a commitment to continuous learning throughout their aviation careers.
**6. Always Prioritise Pre-Flight Preparation**
I will diligently check weather, NOTAMs, and my aircraft's condition before every flight,
ensuring I am fully prepared for safe operations.
**7. Adhere Strictly to Flight Regulations**
I will follow all airspace rules, flight procedures, and regulations,
understanding that these are designed to keep everyone safe.
** Why I’m Making This Pledge**
By publicly pledging, I acknowledge that:
-**Safety is not just my responsibility — it’s an expectation.**\
 Every member of the aviation community looks to me to uphold these commitments.
-**Public commitment matters.**\
 By making my commitment public, I demonstrate to myself and others\
 my resolve to prioritise safety in all aspects of my flying.
-**Together, we set the standard.**\
 By joining other pilots in making this commitment,\
 I help reinforce a culture of safety and professionalism that benefits us all.
