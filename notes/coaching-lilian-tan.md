**April 30, 2024**

Based on the conversation transcript, the main problem Lilian started with was that she was considering joining a media startup company run by her former colleague Mira. However, the job scope and working arrangements had recently changed from what was initially discussed, making Lilian hesitant about taking the role.

Key insights from the conversation:

1. Evaluating the viability and challenges of the video production/news content business model, especially with the disruption from AI technology.
2. Considering alternative paths like positioning herself as a producer for content creators who need help with production, editing, social media promotion etc.
3. Looking at the industry changes from the perspective of understanding and solving users' needs, rather than just creating more content.
4. Exploring different revenue models like taking cuts from sales, charging retainers/coaching fees etc. instead of traditional employment.
5. Thinking deeply about what true "success" looks like for her in this career transition phase.
6. The importance of clearly defining expectations, success criteria and putting provisions like time-bound contracts when entering new business arrangements.

Overall, the conversation helped Lilian gain a broader perspective on the rapidly evolving media landscape and guided her to re-evaluate her options through the lens of user needs rather than just production capabilities.
