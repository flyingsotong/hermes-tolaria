- 10:00am met with Darshini Kandasamy

 - I saw Darsh this morning. We had breakfast together. It had been a long time since we last met, which was in November at Beta. It was good to see her again. February has been a particularly hard month for her. It marks the second anniversary of Mark's passing, and her dad also passed away in February, coinciding with her mom's birthday. We discussed the challenges she's faced communicating with her family about her feelings during the grieving process. We spent a lot of time talking about grief, how she's coping and finding ways to embrace it. It's been a long journey for her.

We also talked about the closure of Between the Lines. Some people saw it as a failure, but that's not the case. We reflected on our time in the industry and realized we both feel ready to move on. She has applied for the GSK fellowship, which, if she gets it, means spending more months in the media industry. At this point, she doesn't feel connected to it anymore and wants to leave.

- 1:30pm met with @L[Lars Bestle](Lars%20Bestle.md)
