# Coaching - Kharishar Kahfi (Kuka)

**April 14, 2025**

**What Kharishar wanted to achieve:**

- Felt stuck in his career
- Frustrated with his job at the Jakarta Post, where he felt he couldn't pursue his passion for science journalism
- Wanted to find a way to do more science reporting and potentially freelance for international outlets

**What Kharishar discovered:**

- Self-limiting beliefs: he realized he was limiting himself by identifying solely as an Indonesian journalist, which prevented him from seeing himself as someone who could work with international media outlets.
- Fear of rejection: he acknowledged his fear of pitching stories to editors and facing potential rejection, which was holding him back from pursuing freelancing.
- Need for action: he recognized that he needed to take proactive steps to change his situation, rather than waiting for things to improve on their own.
- Time management: he identified the possibility of dedicating small amounts of time each day to pursue his goals, even with his busy schedule.

**What I could have done better:**

- Could have explored time management strategies in more detail — for example, helping him create a sample weekly schedule that included dedicated time for freelancing activities.
- Could have helped him set specific, measurable goals related to his freelancing aspirations, giving him a clearer roadmap and increased accountability.
- Could have explored his values to help him connect his desire for change with his deeper motivations.
- Could have inquired more about his support system — identifying people who could offer encouragement, advice, or connections.
- Could have asked him to identify his strengths and skills that could be transferable to freelancing, helping him recognize his existing capabilities and boost his confidence.
