# OSF AIJC in Turin

Day 1 4/15/2024

- AI proxies to make decisions on your behalf

- Power and value flows to the person/company who knows your needs before you do

- Human creation and authentication becomes a luxury good

- Covid showed what an information polluted space would look like

- Information/data poor societies will be left out — fewer positive outcomes

- Information created by non-information class

- Explosion of information requires AI-led processing for insights

- Journalism is a process, not a product

- We assume that AI is tech-led — also consider other megatrends such as climate

- Let's not assume that journalism was always on the right side of history — we haven't been at the forefront of social change
