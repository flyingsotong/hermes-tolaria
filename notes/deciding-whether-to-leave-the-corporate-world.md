1⃣ Do you have financial security, such that you don’t need to return to the corporate world? Be honest with yourself about your target burn rate (and the wealth needed to sustain that).

2⃣ If you had 2x your current wealth, would your lifestyle change and if so how? This question tests if you have truly attained your desired level of wealth, or if you still want more .

3⃣ Do you have any unrealized professional achievement that is really important to you? In other words, are there any other professional mountains that you need to climb?
