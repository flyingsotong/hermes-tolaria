1. The pandemic has rushed the digital transformations in all industries. Remote work and automation are made possible by tech. Opportunity: Greater ability to hire widely, introducing more diversity across more locations. Also, greater competition for talent and retention.

2. Shock to advertising revenue has meant that newsrooms are now more open to testing other sources of income. Subscriptions, memberships, virtual events, research. Opportunity: New skills in sales, marketing, partnerships, tech are needed.

3. Tech platforms are catalyzing the creator economy -- new formats, new forms of distribution, built-in monetization tools. Net effect is the creation of more independent creators in this space. Digital media is increasingly niche. Opportunity: Testing new platforms, reaching new audiences, learning from creators already in this space.
