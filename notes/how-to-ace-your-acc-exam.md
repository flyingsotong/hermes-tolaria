# How to ace your ACC exam

- Establish and maintain agreements. Focus on the client's chosen topic and work together to set clear goals and expectations.

- Cultivate trust and safety. Be present and attentive, and focus on the client's perspective. Be supportive and respectful, and avoid imposing your own views or opinions.

- Maintain presence. Be fully engaged in the conversation and focus on the client's needs. Avoid distractions and interruptions, and be responsive to the client's feedback.

- Listen actively. Pay attention to what the client is saying and ask clarifying questions. Reflect back what you hear to ensure understanding.

- Evoke awareness. Ask open-ended questions to help the client explore their own thoughts, feelings, and beliefs. Avoid giving advice or telling the client what to do.

- Facilitate client growth. Help the client identify their own goals and create a plan to achieve them. Support the client in taking action and reflecting on their learning.

In short, be a good listener, be supportive, and help the client achieve their goals.
