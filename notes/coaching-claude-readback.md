**General questions often missed:**

- "What's the hardest question you're not asking yourself?"
- "How would you feel about this decision three years from now?"
- "What support do you need to make these changes?"
- "What are you trading off by maintaining the status quo? What's your opportunity cost?"
- "What assumptions are you making that might be holding you back?"
- "What would your future self want you to know right now?"
- "What important change have you not started making yet?"
- "What is the one truth I am avoiding?"
- "What is the first small step you can take immediately?"

**Common insights clients gained:**

1. Recognition that their skills are transferable beyond traditional journalism
2. Permission to explore new paths without guilt
3. Reframing "failure" narratives into learning opportunities
4. Understanding that industry changes are systemic, not personal failures
5. Clarity on next steps and action plans

**Key patterns across sessions**

**1. Career transition from traditional media**

Examples: Julianna Barcela, Aniket Narawad, Ganiu Oloruntade

- Feeling stuck in shrinking journalism industry
- Questioning whether to stay in media
- Seeking validation of their skills
- Looking for new career paths

Key questions:
- "What would need to be true for you to feel confident making this change?"
- "How are you defining success in your current role versus your desired role?"
- "What assumptions are you making about your target industry/role?"
- "What skills do you already have that you're not fully recognizing?"

**2. Media entrepreneurship support**

Examples: Emmanuel Samarathisa, Ankur Paliwal, Kate Delos Reyes

- Starting newsletters/media ventures
- Dealing with financial pressures
- Building confidence as entrepreneurs
- Seeking product-market fit

Key questions:
- "What does sustainable success look like for you?"
- "How will you know if this venture is working?"
- "What support systems do you need to put in place?"
- "What's the smallest version of success that would still feel meaningful?"

**3. Leadership and professional growth**

Examples: Thea Alberto-Masakayan, Rachel Akidi Okwir

- Managing teams and organizational change
- Building confidence in leadership roles
- Developing thought leadership
- Career advancement strategies

Key questions:
- "How do you want people to experience your leadership?"
- "What stories are you telling yourself about your capabilities?"
- "What would you do differently if you knew you couldn't fail?"
- "How does your definition of good leadership limit you?"

**4. Identity and purpose**

Examples: Raastineeda Shaikh, Marwa Ershaidat

- Questioning professional identity
- Seeking meaning in work
- Wrestling with work-life balance
- Processing career disappointments

Key questions:
- "What parts of your current work truly energize you?"
- "How might your perspective change if you removed the 'journalist' label?"
- "What would you attempt if you gave yourself permission?"
- "What values are most important to you in your work?"
