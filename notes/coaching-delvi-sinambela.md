**February 19, 2025**

**What Delvi wanted**

Delvi, who has over 20 years of experience in journalism and TV news, was seeking perspective on her current career transition. She had recently quit her job at MNC and was contemplating starting her own venture. She wanted insights from someone who understood the industry and could help her think through her next steps, particularly given her experience in setting up and managing media projects.

---

**Key insights Delvi gained**

1. She recognized that she was approaching her new venture primarily through the lens of traditional media, limiting her ability to see other opportunities
2. Her default thinking about content as the primary product was constraining her vision — content should be the last consideration, not the first, as it's becoming increasingly commoditized with low barriers to entry
3. Instead of focusing on content creation, she should concentrate on identifying and serving specific needs in the market — for example, positioning herself between companies and investors around sustainability/ESG reporting
4. Her journalism background had led her to define her professional identity too narrowly, limiting her ability to adapt to new opportunities

---

**How I could have done better**

1. Spent more time exploring Delvi's specific strengths and experiences in setting up media operations, rather than moving quickly to challenge her assumptions about content creation
2. When she mentioned her experience with sustainability and business reporting, dug deeper into specific examples to help her better visualize how these skills could translate
3. Sometimes provided direct answers rather than letting Delvi discover insights through her own reflection — could have asked more probing questions
4. After identifying that content shouldn't be the primary focus, could have developed a more concrete framework for how she might evaluate and pursue new opportunities
5. When she expressed her long-term desire to build a media operation, could have explored this more thoroughly rather than pivoting away from it

The conversation successfully helped Delvi begin to reframe her thinking, but with these improvements, it could have provided more practical value for her next steps.
