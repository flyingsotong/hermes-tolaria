# Nugget of Wisdom (Joseph Nguyen)

**On suffering and enlightenment**
Suffering is not in the way of enlightenment. It is the path to enlightenment.

**On duality and choice**
All things, good and bad, can be transmuted into catalysts that expand our consciousness through sheer intention. We do not live in a singularity, but in a world of duality. Everything we can perceive carries both positive and negative qualities; therefore, what you seek is what you will find.

 **On the correlation between disbelief and peace**
The less you believe your mind, the more peace you will find.
