# Coaching - Felista Wangari

**March 10, 2025**

The recording didn't go through because of a glitch on Mac Whisper, so I don't have a transcript. But this turned out to be one really amazing call — I think we broke through even before the time was up.

We talked about her fear and unpacked a lot of the things she has rolled up in her head. The one thing that came up constantly was the need for validation — she was confused because she was afraid to take the first move. We talked about how very often, as media professionals, we don't always have the answers and it's easy to look for the known whenever you want to start something new.

She seemed to find that incredibly revealing. We also talked about her ideas of what success and failure look like. Success for her would be to create a knowledge hub, a resource for personal finance, to build a community, and to work with sponsors in a deeper way. Failure — which I thought was surprisingly clear — was not making that first step. I found this refreshing.
