**Pattern Entry Altitude:** Always enter the traffic pattern**at pattern altitude** at non-towered airports.
**Arrival from Downwind Side (Preferred)**
- Approach the pattern on a 45 angle to the downwind leg.
- Join the pattern at the midfield point of the downwind leg.
**Arrival from Upwind Side**
-**Method 1 (Crossing Midfield):**
 - Announce your intentions.
 - Cross over the midfield at least 500 feet above pattern altitude (1,700 feet AGL at YMBD).
 - If large or turbine aircraft are present, cross at 2,000 feet AGL.
 - Once clear of the pattern (around 2 miles), scan for traffic.
 - Descend to pattern altitude.
 - Turn right to enter the downwind leg at a 45 angle at midfield.
***Method 2 (Midfield Crosswind Entry):**
 - Enter on a midfield crosswind at pattern altitude.
 - Carefully scan for traffic.
 - Announce your intentions.
 - Turn onto the downwind leg.
 -**Caution:** Avoid this method if the traffic pattern is busy.
