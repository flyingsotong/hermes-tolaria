**Biggest trend of the past year**

Our view at Splice is that we need to be supporting the work of media startups and micro newsrooms as leaders in the transformation of this industry. We take the view that this is the**golden age** of media led by**technology**.

More options for relational journalism -- also known as the -- spurred by new features for self-publishing:

- Live audio as a format -- Clubhouse, Twitter Spaces, Facebook, Telegram

- Direct to reader relationships -- Substack

- Direct monetization of that relationship -- Spotify, Apple, Substack, YouTube

Opportunity here is that large newsrooms are laying off people -- and new tools are available at the same time.

The industry has failed to either acknowledge or embrace the emergence of this new class.

**Pros** A wider variety of new, diverse voices, more viable business model

**Cons** Increased challenges in discovering those niche voices at the edges -- asymmetrical

**Recommendations**

1. Identify or establish best practices by engaging with upcoming creators. Learn how that works so you understand what bad actors could do in that space.

2. Identify and support important creators.

3. Close ranks with tech companies on monitoring of emerging spaces.
