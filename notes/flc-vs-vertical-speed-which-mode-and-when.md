Many autopilots have two vertical modes, and they work differently:

* **Flight Level Change (FLC)** holds a *target airspeed* — set it and the airplane pitches to maintain that speed while climbing or descending.
* **Vertical Speed (VS)** holds a *target climb/descent rate* — set 500 feet per minute down and the airplane delivers it.
