# Dronefly.sg — One-Year Umami Analytics Audit

**Period:** Aug 2025 – Jun 2026 (10.5 months)
**Total pageviews:** 9,849
**Total sessions:** 5,744
**Source:** Umami self-hosted (stats.fractals.sg)

---

## Traffic Trend

Sessions grew 86% from post-launch trough (294/mo in Sep 25) to 548/mo (May 26) — slow but steady. Flattened since Feb 26 (465-548 range). Pages per visit stabilized at 1.3-1.5 — single-page info site, one read and done.

## GEO Perspective

- **Singapore dominates at 80.5%** — expected but limits growth. US is #2 at 4.3%, doubling from Jan (19) to Apr (91) then settling back.
- **AI citation traffic is real and growing** — ChatGPT sent 20 total visits, tripling from 2/mo (Jan) to 7/mo (Apr). Perplexity (5), Gemini (4), Claude (1). For a Singapore-regulation niche site, this is meaningful GEO signal with zero optimization.
- **Reddit is the #2 acquisition channel** (~624 pageviews in 2026) — roughly tied with Bing + DuckDuckGo + Yahoo combined. Reddit distribution strategy working.
- **Zero crawlable sub-pages for LLMs** — interactive checker, FAQ, penalties, all behind JS hash fragments (#checker, #faq). Completely invisible to GPTBot/ClaudeBot.

## Intent Analysis

- **Registration is the only real content page** — 84% of non-homepage traffic goes to the registration guide. Everything else is anchor-linked sections.
- **B-RID article is dead** — 11 total pageviews, all in Sep 25, zero since. B-RID is Singapore's Remote ID mandate and the biggest regulatory change for drone owners, but the dedicated article is invisible.
- **"Where to fly" intent unserved** — checker (84/mo faq: 80/mo) shows people want location-specific info but no dedicated page exists.
- **FAQ engagement is real but informational-only** — 80 pageviews/mo, people get their answer and leave. No next-step path.

## Structural Flags

- **Mobile is primary platform** (55%) — regulation reference consumed on phone more than desktop.
- **Traffic plateau since Feb 26** — 5 straight months at 465-548 sessions. No new content added, no new search surfaces.
- **No newsletter/community conversion visible** — zero utm-tagged traffic. Newsletter form and Discord invite are on-site but not tracked.

## Key Action Signal

**The B-RID page has zero traffic.** This is the highest-leverage fix. B-RID is the regulatory change every drone owner in Singapore needs to know about, but the article is invisible to both search engines and LLM crawlers. Fixing indexing, internal linking, and making it LLM-crawlable unlocks both organic growth and immediate affiliate revenue (module recommendations with tracked links).
