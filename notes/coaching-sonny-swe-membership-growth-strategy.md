# Coaching - Sonny Swe – Membership Growth Strategy

**February 29, 2024**

Sonny has been feeling stressed lately due to the many things going on. He is trying to reduce costs in a dry year for grants in his space. To do this, he gathered his team around to discuss what could be done. As a result, several jobs may be cut. We had a good chat about this and the opportunities available to him. His best opportunity lies in doubling his membership numbers. Splice has written out a plan for him, and there is a huge opportunity to focus on the low-hanging fruit to drive memberships up. This should be his top priority this year.

We also talked about Sonny's funding needs. Things are quite desperate for him right now. He is moving his family to Chiang Mai for six months, which is a good thing. However, he needs to drastically reduce the costs of his team, which currently stands at 42 full-time staff on payroll. I reminded him that he doesn't need to run all of the products he has out there. If he could just focus on a handful, such as a daily briefing, and do them well, it would be more effective. We also discussed how to support my media. I told him about the grant we have from OSF and the opportunity for us to be involved in something. Sonny suggested looking at Exile Hub to see if there is any need that we can fulfill as the evacuation of refugees continues.
