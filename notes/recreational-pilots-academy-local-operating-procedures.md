**Murray Bridge (Pallamana) Airfield (YMBD)Airfield Information:**
- **ICAO Code:** YMBD
- **Elevation:** 180 ft AMSL (Above Mean Sea Level)
- **Runway 19/01 Length:** 1065 m (3494 ft)
- **Runway 09/27 Length:** 910 m (2985 ft)
## Operating Procedures:
1. **Circuit Direction:** Left-hand circuits are standard for all runways. (Right-hand circuits available at Rollos).
2. **Circuit Altitude:** Minimum 500 ft AGL (Above Ground Level); Normal circuit altitude is 1,000 ft AGL.
3. **Runway Holding:** Do not hold within active runway areas (i.e., inside the white cones).
4. **Runway Visibility:** Be aware that aircraft taking off from Runway 27 are not visible from the threshold of Runway 19. Use radio for clearance.
5. **Joining Procedures:** Preferred circuit joins are via the Crosswind or Downwind legs. Straight-in approaches should be avoided unless necessary (Reference: CAR 166 Para 3).
6. **Glider Operations:** When gliders are operating in the circuit, remember they have right of way.
7. **Aerobatics (D285):** The YMBD CTAF includes the D285 Civil Aerobatics area, which extends up to a 3 NM radius and 4000 ft AMSL.
8. **Active Aerobatic Box:** When the Aerobatic Box is active:
- Use wide circuit procedures for Runway 19.
* Do _not_ join Midfield Crosswind.
- Join via the Downwind or Base legs.
1. **Solo Training Area:** Solo training must be conducted within the designated training area unless a Solo Navigation exercise is specifically authorised by an Instructor. (Refer to Briefing Rooms for Training Area Limits).
2. **Radio Requirement:** It is preferred that all aircraft operating from Murray Bridge have and use a radio.
3. **Radio Procedures:** Standard radio broadcast procedures for Taxiing, Entering Runway, Joining Circuit, and Inbound must be used as per AIP Gen 3.4.
4. **Restricted Airspace:** Be aware of Restricted Area R289 located east of the Murray River (Consult VTC, VNC, ERC Low charts).
5. **Parachuting Operations:** Parachuting operations occur 7 days a week, approximately 14 NM SSE of YMBD on the northern edge of Lake Alexandrina (Refer to VTC & VNC).
6. **RAAus Manual:** Read and abide by the Recreational Aviation Australia (RAAus) Operations Manual.
7. **Pilot Documentation:** Your Pilot Certificate and Medical Certificate (if applicable) must be carried with you during flight.
8. **Notice Board:** Always read the Notice Board before each flight to familiarize yourself with any changes to operations. If unsure about anything, ask your instructor.
9. **Visiting Pilots:** Visitors should refer to and use the unlicensed aerodrome checklist found in the ERSA (GEN section).
## Frequencies (MHz):
- **YMBD CTAF:** 127.50
- **Outside YMBD CTAF (e.g., Strathalbyn / Mannum):** 126.70
- **Area WEST (Adelaide Radar):** 130.45
- **Area EAST (Melbourne Centre):** 125.30
## Pilot Advice:
- **Clothing:** Wear light clothing (ensure it is warm enough in winter). Sneaker-type shoes are preferred; high heels are not suitable.
## Facility Information:
- **Toilet Access Code:** C 1275
