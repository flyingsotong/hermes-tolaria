**1. What conditions could result if a recreational aircraft was loaded so that the C of G was behind the aft limit?**

a. More difficult to raise the nose on take-off and longer take-off run
b. Higher stalling speeds and improved stability in the pitching plane

**7. If the propeller of a recreational aircraft is replaced with a coarser pitch propeller, the likely effect on performance will be?**

a. **Increased take-off distance, decreased climb performance, improved cruise performance**

**14. What is the recommended treatment for a person suffering from carbon monoxide poisoning?**

a. Administer oxygen, provide plenty of water
b. Advise the pilot to get plenty of rest
c. Eat some charcoal tablets
