# Checks HASELL

Ensure that the aircraft is in a safe condition and that the environment is suitable for performing specific maneuvers. Needs to be done prior to ANY aerial maneuver.


**Height**

Ensure sufficient altitude (2500-4500 feet in this context) to recover from the maneuver.

**Aircraft configuration**

Check that the aircraft is in the correct configuration (e.g., flaps, gear).

1. All lights turned on

2. Fuel Pump is turned on

3. Flaps IF required

4. Carb Heat IF required

5. Confirm Altitude

6. Confirm Heading

**Safety**

Ensure safety measures are in place, such as securing seat belts, harnesses, loose items.

**Engine instruments**

Verify that engine Ts & Ps are reading within the green range (Temperature & Pressure).

**Locality**

Confirm you are in the correct area for training, away from populated areas, restricted areas, aerodomes.

**Lookout**

Perform a left 360 clearing turn to ensure the area is clear of other aircraft.**Clearing turns are only done to the left as overtaking aircraft will overtake from the right.**Actively lookout for any aircraft below/level/above you.
