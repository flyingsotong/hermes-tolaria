# Getting paid to vibe code Inside the new AI-era job (Lazar Jovanovic)

This video features an interview with Lazar Jovanovic, the first "professional vibe coder" at Lovable, who uses AI tools to build products without a traditional coding background. He shares his insights on navigating the AI era, emphasizing that having a non-technical background can be an advantage (9:26) and that clarity in communicating with AI is paramount.
[The rise of the professional vibe coder \(a new AI-era job\)](https://youtu.be/0XNkUdzxiZI?si=vcD3qGw4iXQ1NK7j)
Here's a breakdown of key takeaways and advice for product developers in the AI age:
**What is Professional Vibe Coding?** (0:00)
* It's a job where you leverage AI tools to build internal and external products, focusing on bringing ideas to life quickly and with quality (5:47).
* It involves a wide surface area, supporting various departments with flexible and complementary roles (6:19).
**⠀**Key Principles for Working with AI Tools:****
***Embrace a "delusional" mindset:** Believe that everything is possible until proven otherwise (11:42).
***Prioritize planning over execution:** Spend 80% of your time in planning and chatting with AI and only 20% on executing the plan (13:21).
***Understand AI limitations:** AI has a "context memory window" (15:08), similar to a genie granting only a few wishes at a time. Humans, however, have a "human level limitation" where they are not specific enough (16:54).
***Focus on taste and judgment:** Develop an understanding of what "tasteful," "world-class," and "magical" look like, rather than just "good enough" (17:43). This comes from "exposure time" to quality content and people (18:36).
***Use parallel project approaches:** Start multiple projects with varying levels of clarity (brain dump, more deliberate, and code snippets) to refine your ideas and prompts (21:46).
***Create dynamic context windows with PRDs:** Use Product Requirements Documents (PRDs) as the source of truth for your AI, including tasks, subtasks, and specific requirements (29:30).
***Utilize Markdown files (MD files):** AI learns best from Markdown, so create MD files (e.g., rules.md, agent.md) to guide the AI's behavior and focus, preventing repetition in prompts (44:43).
***Prototype still matters:** Even with AI, prototyping is crucial to clarify thinking and experiment with different approaches (50:57).
**What Product Developers Should Start and Stop in the AI Age:**
**Start:**
***Optimizing for quality and magic:** Move beyond "good enough" because AI makes good enough accessible to everyone (56:50). Focus on producing "world-class" and "magical" experiences.
***Building in public:** This is the fastest path to opportunities and can help you become a professional vibe coder (1:30:10).
***Learning design skills and taste:** Design, including images, fonts, and copywriting, will become increasingly valuable as AI handles more deterministic tasks (58:29, 1:24:37).
***Developing emotional intelligence:** Understanding human nature and real-life dynamics will be crucial as more interactions become AI-generated (1:23:32).
***Spending more time on learning than building:** Deliberately allocate more time to understanding how AI agents think and learn from their outputs (1:38:01).
⠀**Stop:**
***Obsessing over tech stack:** The underlying code (HTML, React, etc.) matters less than the end-user experience (1:37:23).
***Fearing the future of engineering:** Elite engineering will be more critical than ever for maintenance, scaling, and infrastructure (1:00:53).
***Relying solely on your own knowledge when stuck:** Use a "4x4 debugging workflow" that involves reading console logs, prompting the AI to write additional logs, and performing code reviews with tools like Code Interpreter (1:05:14).
***Underestimating the AI's learning capability:** When a problem is fixed, ask the AI how you could have prompted it better to avoid the issue next time. Then, integrate this learning into the AI's rules (1:14:27).
***Ignoring agent output:** Religiously read the agent's output to understand its thought process and learn what's possible (1:15:35, 1:18:24).

Based on the principles discussed in the video, here's how you could reframe your resume to highlight your abilities for the AI age:
Instead of focusing on traditional coding or specific tech stacks, emphasize skills that align with "vibe coding" and leveraging AI:
***Clarity and Communication:**
  ***Crafting precise AI prompts and instructions:** Highlight your ability to articulate complex ideas into clear, actionable prompts for AI tools (0:36, 17:09, 17:56).
  ***Translating vague concepts into defined requirements:** Showcase your skill in transforming initial ideas into detailed product requirements (22:26, 36:56).
***Product Vision and Strategy:**
  ***Strategic product planning with AI:** Emphasize your ability to define "what needs to be true" for a project's success through comprehensive documentation like PRDs and MD files (29:30, 31:49).
  ***User-centric design and journey mapping:** Demonstrate your aptitude for defining user experiences and flows (32:51, 48:24).
***Taste, Judgment, and Quality:**
  ***Developing and applying high aesthetic standards:** Highlight your "taste" and "judgment" in discerning world-class design and user experiences (17:43, 36:12).
  ***Optimizing for "magical" outcomes:** Stress your commitment to delivering exceptional quality rather than just "good enough" (56:50).
***AI-Native Workflow and Adaptability:**
  ***Rapid prototyping and iteration with AI tools:** Showcase your proficiency in quickly launching parallel prototypes to refine ideas and accelerate development (21:46, 50:57).
  ***Debugging and problem-solving in AI-driven environments:** Describe your systematic approach to resolving issues by interpreting AI outputs and guiding the AI (1:05:14).
  ***Continuous learning and adaptability in fast-paced AI landscapes:** Emphasize your ability to quickly learn and integrate new AI capabilities and workflows (1:19:08).
***Collaboration and Cross-Functional Impact:**
  ***Bridging product, design, and engineering functions:** Highlight your ability to facilitate seamless workflows across these converging roles using AI as an amplifier (0:24, 56:01).
  ***Empowering non-technical teams with AI solutions:** Showcase instances where you've enabled others to build and innovate using AI tools (54:08).
By focusing on these areas, you can demonstrate your readiness for roles that prioritize conceptual clarity, strategic thinking, and leveraging AI as a powerful building partner, rather than just executing code.
