3 golden rules for news marketing

- First, fear of bothering consumers. not running a marketing campaign because you're afraid of losing people doesn't stack up

- Second, get editorial involved. People are buying into the journalism.

- Third, test everything. There's always scope for testing -- pricing, messaging.
