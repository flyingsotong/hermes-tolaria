* **Fuel:** Sufficient quantity, pump status, and proper tank selection.
* **Radio:** Correct frequencies for ATC or navigation set, volume, and squelch.
* **Engine:** Temperatures and pressures (T's & P's) in the green, carburetor heat check.
* **Direction Indicator (DI):** Aligned with the magnetic compass.
* **Altitude/Altimeter:** Correct altitude and QNH (pressure setting).
