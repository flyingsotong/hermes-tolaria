# Lite Air: Radio Calls

* **Aldinga Traffic**, Piper Sport 8108, turning Base Runway 21 for (Touch and Go or Full Stop), **Aldinga**
* **Aldinga Traffic**, Piper Sport 8108, departing Downwind Runway 21, on climb 2500, tracking southwest to the training area, **Aldinga** (note: not climbing "to" 2500 ...but just climbing 2500)
* **Aldinga Traffic**, Piper Sport 8108, 10 miles to the Southwest, 3,500 inbound, estimating overhead in 7 mins at time 38 (utc), **Aldinga**
* **Aldinga Traffic**, Piper Sport 8108, departing overhead, 1300 climbing, 3500 tracking 110 for the Milang Training Area, **Aldinga**
* **Aldinga Traffic**, Piper Sport 8108, overhead, descending dead side to join crosswind Runway 03, **Aldinga**
