# Presentation Outline Why Media is Failing

**Topic:** The systemic failures and challenges facing modern media.

**Key Areas to Cover:**

1.**Economic Pressures:** The shift from print/broadcast to digital, the decline of advertising revenue, and the rise of paywalls.

2.**Erosion of Trust:** The impact of partisan media, 'fake news,' and the perception of bias.

3.**The Attention Economy:** How clickbait, sensationalism, and the need for constant engagement compromise journalistic integrity.

4.**Platform Dominance:** The role of social media and tech giants in content distribution and monetization.

5.**Local News Crisis:** The disappearance of local reporting and its effect on civic life.

**Goal:** To analyze the structural issues and propose potential solutions for a more sustainable and trustworthy media landscape.
