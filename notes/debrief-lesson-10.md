- In an engine out, immediate get 70 kts right as fast as I can

- Know the effects of flaps 1 and flaps 2

- Only pass traffic at 1,700. No less

- In teardrop turn, make the 45 degree turn early, and start decent to 1,200

- Cross midfield only at 1,200 — no more or less

- When departing circuit, no need to level — just climb directly to assigned altitude

- Get decent right — do not pitch down!

- Crosswind turns — never turn less than 500AGL (700MSL)

- Entry into circuit from active side: Overfly the active side at 500Ft above whatever your circuit altitude is.

- Low level circuit low pass altitudes:

 - FE + 500 ft AGL = 700 ft\
 FE + 200 ft AGL = 400 ft\
 FE + 50 ft AGL = 250 ft

- Normal circuit attitude FE + 1000 ft AGL = 1200
