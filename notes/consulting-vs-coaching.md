client: Consulting vs Coaching
---

Hire a consultant when you need to execute a process or a method. Hire a coach when you want to learn to learn more about how you think and feel.

**Coaching**
- Pulls answers out of you
- You're the driver
- Creates awareness of how you think and feel
- Person focused
- Coach as a mirror, client is the expert
- Awareness is the goal
- Explores possibilities _with _the person

**Consulting**
- Pushes strategies to you
- An execution partner
- Solves a business problem
- Business focused
- Consultant as a subject matter expert
- Execution is the goal
- Designs possibilities _for _the business
