**Directory Overview**

This directory is a professional personal knowledge base, optimized for [Obsidian](https://obsidian.md/). It contains a curated collection of notes on entrepreneurship, coaching, media, aviation, and technology.

**Folder Structure & Organization Logic**

The vault is organized into functional categories. Follow this structure for consistency:

*   **📂 AI:** Artificial Intelligence, LLMs, prompting, and tech-future trends.
*   **📂 Book:** Curated summaries, notes, and highlights from books.
*   **📂 Coaching:** Resources for executive and life coaching, ICF competencies, and frameworks.
*   **📂 Drone:** UAV operations, UAPL licensing, CAAS regulations, and drone theory.
*   **📂 Entrepreneurship:** Startup advice, VC funding, business strategy, and company building.
*   **📂 Flying:** General aviation, pilot training, aircraft manuals (e.g., Jabiru), and flight logs.
*   **📂 Journal:** Chronological daily logs and dated reflections (Format: `Day, Month Date, Year`).
*   **📂 Media:** Journalism trends, creator economy, newsroom strategy, and industry analysis.
*   **📂 Person:** Bios, profiles, and meeting notes for specific individuals.
*   **📂 Personal:** Private reflections, health, finance, and personal logistics.
*   **📂 Splice:** Operational strategy and event notes specific to Splice Media.
*   **📂 Readwise:** Automated exports and synced highlights.
*   **📂 Malay / Post / Exam:** Specialized folders for language, published posts, and pilot certifications.

**File Naming & Formatting Conventions**

To maintain a distraction-free and professional reading experience, the following vault-wide standards are enforced:

**1. Filenames**
*   **Natural Language:** Use spaces between words (e.g., `Ai Impact Series`). No `CamelCase` or `under_scores`.
*   **Emoji-Free:** Filenames are pure text for maximum searchability and system compatibility.

**2. Clean Content**
*   **No Hashtags:** Use `wiki-links` or folder organization instead. Hashtags (e.g., `#Drone`) have been removed.
*   **Emoji-Free Content:** Notes are text-only to ensure a clean, professional aesthetic.
*   **Link Hygiene:** Only direct URLs and functional wiki-links are allowed. Search redirects and export remnants (like `Loc`) are removed.
*   **Template Cleanliness:** Empty placeholders (e.g., `Title:`, `Email:`, `Description:`) are cleared if unused.
*   **Markdown Standards:** Orphan artifacts (like `**
**` or empty bold markers) and decoded HTML entities are eliminated.

**Key Themes**

*   **Entrepreneurship & Media:** Building viable, user-first media businesses.
*   **Coaching & Growth:** Psychological frameworks and professional development.
*   **Aviation:** Mastery of both general aviation and unmanned aircraft (drones).
*   **Technology:** Harnessing AI and automation for meaningful output.
