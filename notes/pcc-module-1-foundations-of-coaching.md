Questions need to be

- non judgmental

- open ended -- but you can use closed questions appropriately and deliberately to validate or clarify

- timely and relevant

- short and simple

Coaching needs to work toward something


**Goals** _What would you like to achieve with your time?_ What is the timeline to achieve this goal? What would you like to work towards? Where do you see yourself a year from now?

**Reality** What's hindering you from this goal? What's happening in your life right now? What would it take for you to achieve this goal? What would happen if this problem went away? Where do you see yourself now?

**Options** If time/money/resources were not issues, how would you do this? What would success look like? What would failure look like? What would happen if you failed on the project?

**Will** On a scale of 1-to-10, how confident are you in achieving your goal? Which of these options is most achievable? What is the timeline for you to achieve this? What do you need for this option to work out for you? What needs to happen to make to make this option a reality?
