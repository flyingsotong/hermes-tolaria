# OSF MDIF

**The report needs to deliver a comprehensive landscape analysis of the Media Development Investment Fund (MDIF), focusing on their expanded activities beyond their core investment portfolio.**

The key deliverables include:

1. Description of MDIF's target market and the types of media organizations they serve.

2. Landscape overview mapping MDIF's expanded activities and comparing them with similar organizations or competitors.

3. Feature comparison highlighting MDIF's unique offerings, overlaps with others, and competitive advantages.

4. Participant interviews and ratings to assess the value and impact of MDIF's services from the client's perspective.

5. Analysis of MDIF's programs, including:

 - Potential for commercialization of services

 - Possible revenue streams

 - Impact on MDIF's mission and existing beneficiaries

 - Cost-effectiveness and operational complexity

 - Relationship between advisory services, Amplify programs, and the investment portfolio

The overall objective is to understand MDIF's uniqueness, additionality, competitive advantage, and impact, as well as to identify areas for potential development or discontinuation in their next strategic phase.

The report should draw on existing data and evaluations while providing fresh insights through new analysis and stakeholder interviews. The final recommendations should help position MDIF's capacity building, strategic advisory, and coaching services in relation to their investment pipeline and overall work.

*Key assumptions to test in this research:

1.**MDIF's uniqueness: Is MDIF truly offering services that are not available elsewhere in the market?**

2.**Additionality: Are MDIF's expanded activities creating value that wouldn't exist without their intervention?**

3. Competitive advantage: What specific factors give MDIF an edge over similar organizations?

4. Impact definition: How do clients define and measure MDIF's impact, and does this align with MDIF's own metrics?

5. Market viability: Is there a commercially viable market for any of MDIF's services without compromising their mission?

6. Program efficiency: Are the expanded activities cost-effective relative to their impact?

7. Pipeline effectiveness: How effectively do the advisory and Amplify programs feed into the investment portfolio?

8. Mission alignment: Do the expanded activities support or potentially detract from MDIF's core mission?

9. Scalability: Can MDIF's successful approaches be scaled up or replicated in other contexts?

10.**Future relevance: Are MDIF's current services positioned to meet the evolving needs of media organizations in deteriorating global environments?*

Our approach:

1. Identify needs-served

2. Identify outcomes-achieved

3. Identify future relevance
