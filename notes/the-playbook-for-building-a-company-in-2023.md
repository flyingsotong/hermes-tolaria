# The playbook for building a company in 2023

<https://twitter.com/gregisenberg/status/1593084240308174852>
**Smart summary:** The main takeaways from the thread are that in order to build a successful company in 2023, one must first build an audience or community, and then build the product. This is a shift from the traditional mindset of building the product first and then finding the audience.
The playbook for building a company in 2023:
Short form video Short form video + newsletter Short form video + newsletter + free community Short form video + newsletter + free community + paid community Short form video + newsletter + free community + paid community + product
TLDR;
Old: build the product and then find the audience/community
Now: build the audience/community and then build the product
Small mindset shift, big difference
