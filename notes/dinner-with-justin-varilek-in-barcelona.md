# Dinner with Justin Varilek in Barcelona

Thu, March 14th, 2024

- 7:00pm met with Justin Varilek in Barcelona
- Had dinner with Justin; it was the first time we had seen each other since 2017 when we were at the Online News Association meetup in Washington DC. A lot has happened since that time. He moved from Berlin to London to Barcelona and now has a son, also called Leo, who is about 3 years old.
- Justin has been going through a very difficult divorce and is currently in a custody fight. The case will be decided on 4/4/2024, so I need to remember to check in on him at that point. He now works as a consultant at Pepsi, where he helps them figure out their entrepreneurial needs. All in all, a really great guy, and it was wonderful to see him again, although I feel incredibly sad for what he's going through.
