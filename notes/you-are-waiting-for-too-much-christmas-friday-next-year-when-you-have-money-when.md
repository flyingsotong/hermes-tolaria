# You are waiting for too much Christmas, Friday, next year, when you have money, when love arrives, when everything is perfect...

Listen, perfection does not exist.

A human being cannot attain it because we are simply not made to be fulfilled here.

Here, we are given an opportunity to learn.

(Mistakenly attributed to Pope Francis — but origin unclear)

Take the risk while you still can.

Till the day you die, all defeats are psychological.


Been following and loving your flying journey. Wanted to share with you a short story which I thought of when you posted initially about struggling. 

When I was in my early 20s I was a nationally competitive player in Halo (first-person shooter). I made it to the finals. At the finals, there was just one player who decimated all the rest of us in a free for all deathmatch.

We finished the game, lights came on, and where most of us where dudes in our 20s, the winner was a 9 year old. When I asked about it, like with all my experience and playtime and everything how could this kid beat me, someone explained, he sees the game different. He isn’t bound by the rules because he hasn’t learned them. 

That early on, I knew: kids (people younger than me) were gonna beat me, and so I needed to be listening to them and teaming up with them; and that i had to stay flexible. So watching your journey just reminded me of that. You got beat, those kids did it better and faster. But you hung on and you’re still in the game. I am a fan as always!

Un-learning is just as important as learning. We are compounded by the stories we’ve told ourselves about ourselves over the years. Some may no longer be true.
