# Byron Perry, founder and CEO, Coconuts

Coconuts just turned 10.

The online publisher, which puts out city guides across Bangkok, Manila, Singapore, Hong Kong, Kuala Lumpur, Jakarta, Bali, and Yangon, has been pushing forward into new formats such as broadcast and streaming platforms.

We spoke with Byron Perry, the founder and CEO of Coconuts about the decade of lessons he's had in building one of Asia's most recognizable media startups.

We picked his brains about fund raising, branding, expansion strategies, and growing talent.

Hosted by Splice's co-founders [Rishad Patel.md](Byron%20Perry,%20founder%20and%20CEO,%20Coconuts/Rishad%20Patel.md) and Alan Soon.
