Meeting: Tuesday March 17, 2009 With John Casey on management tips

Building trust:

1. Integrity

2. Intention

3. Capability

4. Results

- Keep addressing their concerns

- Don't play up the journo stuff

- Dialogue must be about what they want

- Who will I win over in 30 days?

- Who do I need to win over?

- Core strength is useless as the start

- Find out what they want right away

- Demand to know what I am expected to achieve, ask for specific targets

- Prevent your mission statement from getting hijacked

- Get everyone on the same page

- Find out what's my mandate/authority

- Don't be a pushover; don't be weak

- Give aggressive feedback. Always come up with development needs alongside company's needs

- Don't let people's development hijack the business

- Be in love with the viewer

- If you know their jobs, you will know their problems

- What is my boss most afraid of me doing?

- Have systematic, regular meetings with people in the region

- Always send out agenda before meetings

- What is the demo?

- Fight the newsroom urge

- Control your communications if you feel you don't have all the answers

- Find the most difficult part of the business and be great at it
