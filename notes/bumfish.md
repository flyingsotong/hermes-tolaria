- **Brakes:** Checked and off/set for landing
- **Undercarriage:** Down and locked (or checked fixed)
- **Mixture:** Set rich/full
- **Fuel:** Sufficient quantity, pump on, proper tank selected
- **Instruments:** Engine gauges (temp/pressure) checked
- **Switches:** Landing light and magneto check
- **Hatches/Harnesses:** Secure
