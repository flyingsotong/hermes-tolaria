# Splice is home to a community of media professionals transforming the industry in Asia.

The transformation of media begins with getting the definitions right. In 2021, media is no longer just news, just editorial, just entertainment, or just sports.

Media is defined

Through the processes taken to reach its users.

Content creation Content distribution Content amplification Content monetization Content IP

By its formats.

Articles Social posts Social stories Short video Long video Documentary Images Illustrations Podcasts Audio rooms Newsletters

So when we talk about the ecosystem, we're referring to the players who are either directly or indirectly involved in the creation of content, how it's delivered, how it's sustained,

Accelerators Advertisers Book publishers Community support Consultants Data tools NGOs Foundations Freelancers Gamers Investigations CDNs Press freedom supporters Broadcasters Social media VCs Search Portals PR Website developers e-Commerce Schools

We started Splice in 2015 because we wanted to transform this space through a more inclusive, more optimistic view of media. We believe we're in the Golden Age of media.

Our dream is to be at the center of this transformation in Asia.

We...

Report Train Celebrate Fund Advocate

We work in several formats

Newsletters Articles Podcasts Audio rooms Events Social posts

...and data

Introducing Splice Meta, our still-secret product to map and identify the gaps in the media ecosystem.


What we know to be true in 2021

- Attention spans are shorter -- you're competing against single app for attention

- Content needs to be targeted -- don't count on people stumbling on it

- Niche content matters more than mass

- Communities are a collection of fans. You need to be at the center of that to have influence

- The barriers to entry are lower than ever
