# FPU coaching 2023 Oonib Azam

**26 October 2023**

- wants to start a data-driven journalism outfit -- but doesn't know where to begin

- talked him through personas -- the people he actually knows who've benefitted from his work so far, what was the value created, how did that help them?

- his insight: you can make money in journalism if you don't call it journalism -- sell the insights, help people make decisions confidently

- my insights: I was quite happy with how this went -- the thought flow, the flow of insights. More consulting than coaching, but perhaps I've found that comfortable overlap

Examples cited: [https://site.thibi.co/](https://site.thibi.co) [https://www.cbinsights.com/](https://www.cbinsights.com) [https://www.statista.com/](https://www.statista.com)

I started my career as a reporter with English daily, The Express Tribune in November 2014. My career mostly revolves around reporting for the print media, but I have also worked with one of the leading Urdu news channels of Pakistan, Samaa for about one year. I have also worked for a local English news channel Tribune 24-7 for about 10 months. Currently, I am associated with The News International as a city desk reporter. Alongside my reporting for English dailies, I also work for the digital desk of my organization which involves video stories and podcasts.

I have majorly covered civic issues, local government, transport and environmental issues of Karachi. Recently, I have covered some very sensitive land grabbing stories, in depth, for my paper. One of my stories on land grabbing has also been selected among top 10 data driven stories by Global Investigative Journalism Network.
