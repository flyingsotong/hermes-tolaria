- 10:00am met with Hon Yuen Leong for introductory

 - A middle, late-career journalist who's looking for flexibility in her day as she's caring for an elderly mom doesn't want the traditional path of working in newsrooms. Unfortunately, she defines herself very much as a news producer.

 - We talked about theories of change and where the industry is going. I discussed the need for us in the industry to widen and broaden our definition of what news and media are, and in the process, figure out new opportunities to serve audiences.

 - We talked about creators and the need for credible creators in the information ecosystem, and therefore the opportunities ahead of her to make that happen. Overall, I felt she has low energy and low positivity despite the work she's done before. She says she's never tried TikTok, doesn't understand AI, and doesn't understand data journalism despite having attended workshops on it. She strikes me as someone who's never sought out these opportunities or looked for ways to upgrade herself over the years and is now in a much more difficult position in trying to figure out how she wants to live.
