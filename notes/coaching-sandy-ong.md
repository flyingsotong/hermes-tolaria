# Coaching - Sandy Ong

**March 27, 2025**

Sandy is a freelance journalist who has been working independently since 2017. She reached out to explore her career direction, particularly in light of industry changes and personal uncertainty.

---

**Key themes explored**

Industry transformation: declining newsroom budgets, changing consumer news consumption habits, increasing role of AI in content creation, and a shift from traditional journalism to expertise-driven content.

Sandy's challenges: fluctuating workload, uncertainty about future career direction, feeling vulnerable as a solo freelancer, desire to remain adaptable and relevant.

Potential strategies discussed:

- Reframing professional identity from "freelance journalist" to "science insights expert"
- Developing a strong personal brand
- Leveraging AI tools for workflow optimization
- Exploring alternative revenue streams and skill development

---

**Key insight: from process to value**

Stop selling the "journalism process." Start selling expertise and insights. Focus on the value created, not the method of creation.

On AI: use it as a co-thinker and analysis tool, learn prompt engineering, explore it for workflow optimization and reflection.

---

**Coaching strengths and missed opportunities**

Effective strategies: asked open-ended, reflective questions; provided industry perspective; offered concrete examples; validated Sandy's experiences; encouraged exploration of alternative paths.

Potential missed opportunities: could have more deeply explored Sandy's specific passion areas; might have developed more concrete action steps; could have helped create a more structured two-year transition plan; minimal discussion of a specific skill development roadmap.

---

**Next steps for Sandy**

- Explore SkillsFuture courses
- Experiment with AI tools
- Network with solopreneurs and creators
- Develop a clear two-year career vision
- Refine personal brand around "science insights"
- Diversify content formats strategically

Reflection questions:

- What scientific domains am I most passionate about?
- How can I transform my current skills into unique value propositions?
- What emerging technologies or storytelling methods excite me?
- How can I build a sustainable, flexible professional identity?
- What unique perspectives or skills do I bring that AI or other journalists cannot easily replicate?
