# Coaching - Padmaja Venkataraman

**January 31, 2025**

This call went unrecorded at her request.

Padmaja has been at the BBC monitoring covering China for the last four years, and feels at a bit of a loss in terms of where her career is headed and what she needs to do for the next step of the journey. I walked her through some of the trends she's seeing in this space. We talked a lot about redefining journalism and how that sets you free to explore new options.

She came away feeling a bit lighter and more excited about where her prospects were and what she could do going forward. She also asked a really interesting question about how I came to leave corporate journalism to start my own thing — no one else has expressed that level of curiosity before.
