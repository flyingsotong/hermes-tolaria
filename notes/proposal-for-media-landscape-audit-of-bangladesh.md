# Proposal for Media Landscape Audit of Bangladesh

**Introduction**

The media landscape in Bangladesh is rapidly evolving with the emergence of new creators, startups, and innovative entrepreneurs. Conducting a comprehensive media landscape audit will provide valuable insights into the current state of the media ecosystem, identify key players, and uncover growth opportunities.

**Objectives**

1. To map out the current media landscape in Bangladesh.

2. To identify and profile content creators, media startups, and entrepreneurs.

3. To evaluate the influence of journalism schools, accelerator programs, and mentorship networks.

4. To assess the challenges and opportunities within the media sector.

**Methodology**

1.**Desk Research**: Collect secondary data from industry reports, news articles, and online databases.

2.**Surveys and Interviews**: Conduct surveys and interviews with media professionals, educators, and entrepreneurs.

3.**Field Analysis**: Visit key media organizations, journalism schools, and innovation hubs.

4.**Data Analysis**: Analyze collected data to identify trends, patterns, and key insights.

**Key Components**

1.**Media Creators and Entrepreneurs**:

 - Catalogue active content creators across various platforms.

 - Profile successful media entrepreneurs and their business models.

2.**Media Startups**:

 - Identify and analyze emerging media startups.

 - Evaluate their market impact and innovation potential.

3.**Journalism Schools**:

 - Assess the curriculum and training methodologies.

 - Determine the role of journalism schools in shaping the media landscape.

4.**Accelerator Programs and Incubators**:

 - Review existing media-focused accelerators and incubators.

 - Analyze their contribution to startup growth and media innovation.

5.**Mentorship and Networking**:

 - Map out key mentors and networks supporting media professionals.

 - Evaluate the effectiveness of mentorship in career and business development.

6.**Regulatory Environment**:

 - Examine the legal and regulatory framework governing the media sector.

 - Identify potential barriers and enablers for media enterprises.

7.**Market Dynamics**:

 - Analyze audience demographics, consumption patterns, and content preferences.

 - Study the competitive landscape and identify market gaps.

**Deliverables**

1. A comprehensive report detailing the findings of the media landscape audit.

2. An executive summary highlighting key insights and strategic recommendations.

3. A database of media creators, startups, and entrepreneurs.

4. A strategic roadmap for stakeholders to navigate the media ecosystem.

**Timeline**

-**Phase 1 - Planning and Research Design**: 1 month

-**Phase 2 - Data Collection**: 2 months

-**Phase 3 - Data Analysis and Report Writing**: 1 month

-**Phase 4 - Review and Finalization**: 2 weeks

**Budget**

An itemized budget will be provided upon the determination of the scope and scale of the audit activities.

**Conclusion**

This media landscape audit will serve as a foundational tool for understanding the dynamics of the media industry in Bangladesh, fostering growth, and encouraging innovation within the sector.
