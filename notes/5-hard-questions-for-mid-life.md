1\. How might I let go of the need to be perfect and focus on building genuine connections with others? _(This question helps shift focus from external expectations to meaningful relationships)_
2\. Am I managing my life to fit someone else’s idea of success or am I truly enjoying the journey? _(This question encourages reflection on priorities and values)_
3\. What would happen if I stopped trying to improve myself and instead focused on understanding and accepting who I am? _(This question promotes self-acceptance and authenticity)_
4\. What are the "should" and "must" that are holding me back and what would I do if I followed my true desires? _(This question helps identify external pressures and explore personal aspirations)_
5\. What am I holding onto out of fear or control and what would happen if I allowed myself to feel and be present? _(This question invites exploration of emotional blocks and the possibility of greater freedom and empowerment)_
