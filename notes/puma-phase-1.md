*OUTPUT 4: Increasing Audience Engagement

For this output we will be collaborating with Splice so that we can develop a roadmap toward increased audience engagement. Overall, the end goal will be to build our audience so that it can lead to financial sustainability and this will be our first steps in our quest to do so.

This research and roadmap would help us to see which path, whether increasing audience size so that leads to profitability in ad placements, or membership models which would allow the audience to contribute to PumaPodcast directly, would be best to pursue.

As per our discussions with Splice, we can only know which would work best if we develop a better understanding of our audience. To that end, these are what Splice proposes to execute in support of increasing audience engagement:

**Activity 1: Understanding our value proposition, and increasing our audience engagement**

- To better understand our listeners, we will conduct a review of our listeners with Splice. Specifically, we want to know if our users' affinity is to the Puma brand, or the individual podcasts themselves.

- We will work with Splice on a set of three 1-hour focus group sessions. Puma will arrange the meetings and select the attendees. Splice will run the interviews and write up the summary and suggestions for our next steps.

- The findings from the review will inform the next steps of our audience strategy, and in particular, whether it is necessary to take the potential future step of hiring of a community manager to further deepen the loyalty of our listeners or whether this can be integrated into the tasks of the existing staff members.

- The review will take place in September 2021 with the action report delivered by mid-October. A written report will be developed by Splice, detailing the process and findings of the review and three focus group sessions and include a conclusion and direction of next steps of an audience strategy to be taken by Puma Podcast.

** ****Splice budget Phase 1** Focus group sessions: 3 x 1-hour sessions = 3 hours Focus group sessions pre- and post- work, including report writing = 3 hours Total 6 hours = US$6,000 Splice hourly rate for IMS = US$1,000
