**Audit questions**

What are the top three customers that you’ve won, and the top three customers that you’ve lost?

What’s something that would only happen here but wouldn’t at other organizations?

What area of the business would you say this company is behind on, given its stage?

Can you tell me about your founding team’s background and why you’re tackling this particular problem?

How would your team describe you?

If you left this role, what do you think the next generation would do in this position?
