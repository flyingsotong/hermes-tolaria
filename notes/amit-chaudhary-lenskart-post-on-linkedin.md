[#hiring #ai #artificialintelligence #lenskart #executivehiring | Amit Chaudhary | 32 comments](https://www.linkedin.com/posts/4daxis_hiring-ai-artificialintelligence-share-7428542157642223617-GIXx/)

I’m hiring for a role that doesn’t have a traditional job title.

I need a buddy who is AI-native shadow. Someone who’s by my side all day — in meetings, on calls, on the road — whose entire job is to keep me at the frontier of AI.

Let me explain.
I’m a co-founder at Lenskart. I probably understand business. I understand what AI is capable of at a strategic level. But the landscape is moving at lightspeed, and I want someone whose full-time job is to be my plug into the AI world.

Not a consultant I meet once a week.
Not a newsletter I skim over coffee.
A person. In the room. Every day.

Here’s what this looks like:
→ You wake up and know what dropped in AI overnight — new models, tools, papers, startups. You tell me what matters and why.
→ You sit in my meetings and listen through an AI-native filter — constantly connecting what we’re discussing to what’s now possible.
→ You coach me. Push me to go deeper. Hands-on with tools, frameworks, even code. We learn together — you bring the technical depth, I bring the business context.
→ When I ask “can AI do this for our stores, our supply chain, our customers?” — you don’t just say yes or no. You prototype it. You find the right people. You turn curiosity into clarity.
→ You curate, not flood. I don’t need 50 links. I need 3 things that matter, with your take on why.

Who you are:

You’re obsessively curious about AI — foundation models, agents, computer vision, NLP, open-source, hardware — the full landscape. You can hold your own with a researcher but you speak in plain language. You’re ego-light and impact-heavy. You’ve probably built something with AI, not just read about it.

This isn’t an EA role (though there’s overlap). It’s an intellectual partnership. You’ll have a front-row seat to how decisions get made at a newly-public company — and your job is to make my thinking sharper, faster, and more AI-native every single day.

To apply — don’t just send a resume.
Send me:
1. A 2-min Loom video on something happening in AI right now that most people are sleeping on — and why it matters for a company like Lenskart
2. One thing you’ve built or deeply explored using AI
3. Why you’re the right shadow

Drop it in DMs or comments. Let’s go.
