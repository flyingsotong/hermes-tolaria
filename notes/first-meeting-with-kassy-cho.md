# First Meeting with Kassy Cho

Mon, January 29th, 2024

- 11:00am met with Kassy Cho

 - I met with Kassy for the first time today, which is quite strange because she's been running her channel for about three years now, although at different speeds. Her background is in digital journalism. She worked at BuzzFeed in the UK for several years and has been trying to figure out how to tell stories to an Instagram audience. They are primarily on Instagram and TikTok, which is great to see. Cassie is a New Zealand native, interestingly, and grew up in Taiwan and the UK. She currently has three people on her staff, with the goal to keep growing. She didn't have much to say about her expansion plans, except that there was a need for it in Taiwan, which is true. She doesn't see any competitors in this space, especially regarding international news. For example, she's been covering Gaza quite a bit because there's not much coverage in Taiwan about that. She has an English and a Chinese channel, which is good to see. Apart from that, there wasn't much else that came out of the call. However, I'm excited to see how we could use her in Splice Beta. This could be one of the discoveries for this year. It doesn't sound like she is very well connected in the media space, so that would be an opportunity for us. I hope to have her on Splice Pink at some point.
