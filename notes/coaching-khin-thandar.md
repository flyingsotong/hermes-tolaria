**December 1, 2023**

Today's session was a nice round-up to what's been a full year of coaching with Thandar. She's been in her new role for about three weeks now and was feeling a bit stressed about everything that's been going on this year — and ultimately the feeling that she has not done enough for herself.

Her insight this time was around how she's been trying to do too much, and wondering at the same time whether she's been fulfilling her own personal goals for the year. I helped her see what she's been doing as part of her work, and how that relates to her desire to be a professional coach.

We also talked about what was achievable from her program standpoint, what is outside of her scope of control, and what is within. For example, her desire is around shaping change for Women in Newsrooms, but often she's starting to see that incentives for change within a newsroom are not aligned. She takes it personally when changes don't materialize.

The takeaway was also around how she pegs her sense of success to other things or other people, when ultimately the important thing is to understand how she's grown.

Her insight: she will take this time to map out what she has taught herself this year — what she has learned and what she has given to a community.

My insight: I should be doing more of these, and not lose the muscle memory around having these conversations.
