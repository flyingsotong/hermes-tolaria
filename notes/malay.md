# Malay

who = siapa

what = apa

where = mana

when = bila

why = kenapa (formal = mengapa)

which one = yang mana (satu)

how = macam mana (formal = bagai mana)

what time = pukul berapa

siapakah nama awak = what's your name (formal)

siapa nama ibu awak = what's your mother's name

siapa nama nenek awak = what is you grandmother's name

kerja awak = your work kerja saya = my work

ayah = bapa = father ibu = emak = mother

tua = old besar = big

box = kotak apa dalam kota tu = what is in that box

what color is your car = apa warn kereta awak?

what time do you go to sleep = pukul berapa awak tidur

tadi = just now

what did you eat just now = apa awak makan tadi

mana = where where you go = mana awak pergi = awak pergi mana

money = duit where's your money = mana duit awak?

alan di mana (formal) alan dekat mana (informal) alan kat mana (informal)

awak kat mana = kat ne (where you at?)

birthday = hari jadi = hari lahir

bila hari jadi awak = when is your birthday

arrive/until = sampai bila awak sampai = when did you arrive

your work = kerja awak you work = awak kerja

my dad = bapa saya

for possessives, "awak" or "saya" goes to the end of the sentence -- the person always goes to the end

my car = kereta saya her house = rumah dia his son = anak dia grandfather's cat = kuching datuk grandma's eye = mata nenek brother's hair = rambut abang younger sister's foot = kaki adik

sad = sedih kenapa awak sedih = why are you sad? kenapa awak marah = why are you angry? kenapa dia ketawa = why is he laughing?

which one is your sister = yang mana satu kakak awak? which is your shirt = yang mana baju awak

macam mana = how macam mana awak pergi kerja = how do you get to work?

your foot is in pain, how are you going to walk = kaki awak sakit, macam mana nak jalan

how much = berapa how much is this fish = berapa harga ikan ini how many times were you late = berapa kali awak lambat

how many rooms are in the house = berapa bilik dalam rumah ni?

who is that = siapa tu who is that person = siapa orang tu

who is in that car = siapa dalam kreta itu (itu is always at the back) who is in that room = siapa dalam bilik itu

what is your name = apa nama awak (form. siapa nama awak -- the only exception)

what's over there = apa di sana

di sini di sana di situ (there... same as "sana")

kotak = box katak = frog katek = short

there is a frog in the box = ada katak di dalam kotak itu

where's my money = mana duit saya where's your car = mana kereta awak which is your car = yang mana kereta awak

saya ibu = i am a mother ibu saya = my mother

kereta saya = car (**that belongs to)**me anak saya = child (that belongs to) me

please come to my house = sila datang ke rumah saya
