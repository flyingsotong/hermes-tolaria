# Is Your Mindset Stuck on Charging Too Little for Coaching (Rhonda Hess)

- Description: Shift your mindset about coaching fees and earn well from the start.

- Rhonda Hess

- Highlights

 -**The downside of offering free or low-cost coaching past coach training is it becomes an ingrained habit.** That’s how some coaches get stuck in what I call _perpetual student mode._

This is where that _practice-integrates-learning_ thing can work against you, as it becomes increasingly difficult to shift from free or charging little to charging fees that pay you well.
