## International Certifications (No Course Required)

### INIFAC Credentials
- **CCF** (Certified Competent Facilitator): 7 documented facilitation sessions, min 2.5/5 competency score
- **CSF** (Certified Skilled Facilitator): Same 7 sessions, higher bar
- **CMF** (Certified Master Facilitator): 30 sessions (15 in-person), 5 reference letters, min 4.0/5

No mandatory course — submit sessions + written application + (CMF) video of yourself facilitating.
FNS FiT workshop strengthens an INIFAC application.

**Provider:** https://inifac.org
**SG partner:** Facilitators Network Singapore (FNS)
