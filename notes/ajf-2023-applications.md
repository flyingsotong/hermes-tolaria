_Thank you for agreeing to be on the Asia Journalism Fellowship’s selection committee again this year. We received a big response, with 121 applicants, the highest number in several years. The programme will run for eight weeks in July and August, four weeks online followed by four weeks in Singapore. _
_You know AJF well, we are looking for journalists who have been doing well, display a passion for what they do, and hopefully will remain in the profession to make a difference in their communities. Aside from samples of their work and awards won, the input of referees who clearly know the candidates well often helps in assessing the finalists. This year we asked applicants to indicate an area they will be happy to make a presentation on when they come to Singapore. Not everyone understood the question, but we want to keep the Fellows’ presentations a key part of the programme. _
