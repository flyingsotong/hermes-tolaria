**Open ended coaching**

1. Track their changes, milestones

2. Celebrate their wins, help them instill that memory of progress

3. Draw attention to what's new -- show the effects of coaching

4. Be the biggest cheerleader for your client

**Outcome-based coaching**

1. Session 1: Discovery -- what's the outcome?

2. Session 2: Why hasn't that outcome been achieved?

3. Sessions 3-8: Reorganize their minds -- help them let go

4. Sessions 9-10: What behaviors do we need to change?

5. Session 10: Review progress -- how does this feel?

6. Session 11-12: How do we create a long-term impact?
