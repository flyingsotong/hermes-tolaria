**August 1, 2023**

She wrote: "How about let's start with 'I feel like I'm stuck in my career with nowhere to go. Should I stay in journalism or go elsewhere?'"

- Beliefs: I can only do this kind of work (documentary producer) here at Mediacorp; I need to learn other things before I can move on
- I want to get promoted — it tells me I'm recognized
- I want to win an award — it tells me I'm recognized
- Her insights: I can do this elsewhere; this job isn't going to get better so I need better reasons to stay
- My insights: I'm still struggling with follow-ups — what can I send her?

Questions used:

- "If you called me in 3 years and told me that they'd been the best 3 years of your life — professionally and personally — what would you tell me?"
- What are your assumptions here? "Nowhere to go"...
- What scares you the most right now?
- What opportunities are ahead of you?
- What would be a meaningful career for you?
- What is the change you want for yourself? Is this the change you want for yourself? Ok, let's go there.
- Based on what you've just learned about yourself, what will you need to move into that?
