**February 3, 2025**

**What Patrick got out of it:**

1. Concrete guidance on building an audience by focusing on solving specific needs rather than just creating content.
2. Understanding that at his early stage (~80 subscribers), he shouldn't get too attached to any particular format or style.
3. A key insight about focusing on niche communities as his unique value proposition, building on his successful Roblox article.
4. Clarity on the industry's direction toward fragmentation and niche interests.
5. Permission to pivot and experiment rather than feeling locked into one approach.
6. Practical networking opportunities through the Splice Slack community.
7. A reality check about the diminishing value of prestigious bylines.

**What worked:**

1. Started by breaking the rule of avoiding tactical advice because that's what Patrick needed most at this stage.
2. Asked probing questions to help him think deeper about his subscribers' needs.
3. Provided actionable suggestions like reaching out to his "patient zero" subscriber.
4. Gave honest perspective about the industry's challenges while still offering constructive paths forward.
5. Recognized and built upon Patrick's strengths, particularly his talent for covering niche communities.
6. Offered concrete support through community introductions and future mentoring.
7. Balanced between immediate tactical needs and longer-term strategic thinking.

**Areas for deeper exploration:**

1. His current day job situation and how it impacts his journalism goals
2. His specific timeline or milestones for growing his newsletter
3. What success looks like to him beyond audience growth
4. His personal connection to arts and urban culture coverage — why this particular beat?
5. More about his workflow and content creation process
6. His monetization goals and timeline
7. Potential collaborations or partnerships he could pursue

Overall, an excellent job providing both tactical and strategic guidance while helping Patrick reframe his approach to focus on niche communities as his differentiator.
