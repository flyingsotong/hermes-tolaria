**What is positive stability?**
The tendency for the aircraft to correct itself from a displacement over time

**What is Straight and Level?**
- Maintaining wings level to the horizon
- Not yawing or rolling left or right
- All forces are in balance

**How would you correct a disturbance which puts the aircraft out of balance?**
Use the applicable rudder input

**What does trim do for the pilot?**
It reduces the control column pressure

**What does LPAT stand for?**
Lookout, Power, Attitude, Trim

**During a climb, we need to lower the nose and do a lookout. How often should this be done?**
Every 500 ft

**When we look in, what are we checking?**
Temperatures and Pressures

**When leveling off we use LAPT. What does this stand for?**
Lookout, Attitude, Power, Trim

**What is best angle of climb?**
The most height in the shortest distance
