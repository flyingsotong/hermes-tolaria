# Air Legislation 1 (RAAus Practice Exam)

**Source:** Pilot Practice Exams - RAAus Pre-Cert Air Legislation
**URL:** https://www.pilotpracticeexams.com/exams/raa/raa-pre-cert-legislation-1-pilot-practice-exam/
**Type:** Multiple choice, 20 questions
**License:** Free practice exam

---

1. **When aircraft A is heading 000° at 1000ft and aircraft B is heading 315°, they will collide if they continue at the same speed — then:** aircraft B must give way to A by turning left / none of these / **aircraft A must give way to B by slowing and turning right behind B** ✓ / aircraft B must give way to A by turning right

2. **In a diagram showing two aircraft on converging paths, which method of giving way is most correct?** B should give way by turning left and passing behind A / **A should give way by turning right and then passing behind B** ✓ / B should give way by turning right and passing behind A / A should give way by turning left and passing behind B

3. **An aircraft backtracking on runway 21 would be heading at a bearing of:** none of these / **030°** ✓ / 210° / 090°

4. **A standard circuit pattern is generally flown at an altitude of ____ feet.** 1500 / 500 / **1000** ✓ / 2000

5. **Alcohol must not be consumed by flight crew within ____ hours of departure.** 8 / **12** ✓ / 2 / 10

6. **In a diagram showing two aircraft, which has to give way?** B / **A** ✓ / Both A and B

7. **When overtaking another aircraft:** you should overtake only after asking permission / you should overtake on the left / none of these / **you should overtake on the right** ✓

8. **Which is most true in regards to recreational aircraft having any responsibility to keep constant lookout?** / **Recreational aircraft have a responsibility to keep constant lookout for all other aircraft** ✓

9. **The standard circuit pattern involves:** a series of right and then left hand turns / a series of right hand turns / a series of left and then right hand turns / **a series of left hand turns** ✓

10. **Smoking is prohibited within how many metres of an aircraft that is refuelling?** 10 m / 9 m / **6 m** ✓ / 15 m

11. **In an overtaking diagram: the faster aircraft B should move right and overtake slower aircraft A** ✓

12. **If on a head on collision path with another aircraft:** southbound or eastbound should give way by turning right / both turn left / **both aircraft should give way by turning to the right** ✓ / southbound or eastbound gives way by turning left

13. **In a standard circuit pattern the order of the legs is:** **crosswind, downwind, base** ✓ / base, crosswind, downwind / base, crosswind, upwind / none of these

14. **A licensed aerodrome must have:** at least two Signal Display Areas / at least three / at least four / **at least one Signal Display Area** ✓

15. **When two aircraft are approaching an aerodrome for the purpose of landing:** higher has right of way / higher has right of way only if on left / **the lower aircraft has the right of way** ✓ / lower has right of way only if on left

16. **In regards to a landing aircraft, who has right of way?** **A landing aircraft has right of way over an aircraft wanting to enter and line up for takeoff** ✓

17. **Which statement is most true in relation to an aircraft landing under an emergency situation?** / **Everyone must give way to an aircraft landing under an emergency situation** ✓

18. **If you have the right of way:** none of these / other aircraft must avoid collision / **all pilots have a legal and common sense responsibility to lookout and take evasive action** ✓ / you must avoid other aircraft

19. **Runways are numbered by:** degrees anti-clockwise from south / **degrees clockwise from north** ✓ / degrees clockwise from south / degrees anti-clockwise from north

20. **An aircraft on runway 27 would be taking off from:** **East to West** ✓ / West to East / South to North / North to South
