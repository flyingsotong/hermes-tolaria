# NSC Meeting Summary – Media sustainability – May 4, 2021

**NSC Meeting Summary – Media sustainability – May 4, 2021**

This discussion will explore two core strategies critical to independent media around the world: growing the**financial sustainability of media and improving media regulatory environments** so that independent media can thrive.   The conversation will be off-the-record, so that all participants can speak freely.
