# How to generate and validate startup ideas in 2024 with AI (Greg Isenberg)

<https://twitter.com/gregisenberg/status/1821173742224998437>

1. Scrape AngelList, ProductHunt, and TechCrunch using Bright Data's web unlocker

2. Feed data into Claude and GPT-4 for multi-model trend analysis and gap identification

3. Use Ahrefs to find high-volume, low-competition keywords in emerging niches

4. Leverage Exploding Topics Pro for early trend detection

5. Use Gummysearch to find trending Subreddits (and painpoints, solution requests)

6. Analyze Fiverr API for trending freelance service requests

7. Deploy SerpApi to analyze Google's "People Also Ask" for unmet needs

8. Use Roam Research with Elicit AI integration for enhanced note-taking and research

9. Use SpyFu to find your competitors' most profitable keywords

10. Generate industry-specific ideas with Anthropic's Claude AI

11. Validate concepts with SurveyMonkey's Audience panel

12. Analyze competitors with SimilarWeb's digital marketing intelligence

13. Use Google Patents to scrape recent filings in emerging tech

14. Feed patent data into GPT-4 for plain English summaries

15. Leverage PatSnap's AI for technology trend forecasting

16. Use Semantic Scholar to scrape latest research papers

17. Implement for AI-powered research exploration

18. Use Everlaw's AI to analyze upcoming legislative changes

19. Use Glimpse to track cultural shifts and predict future demand

20. Prototype with Webflow's advanced interactions and animations

21. Test landing pages with Unbounce's Smart Traffic AI

22. Run micro-tests on Reddit and Twitter using Phantombuster

23. Analyze feedback with Viable's AI-powered insights platform

24. Use RapidAPI to analyze trending and newly released APIs

25. Implement ThingSpeak to collect and analyze IoT sensor data

26. Use Midjourney and DALL-E 3 for product concept visualization (or use humans at DesignScientist.com)

27. Leverage Nexus AI for complex system analysis and opportunity mapping

28. Use Premise for real-time, on-the-ground data collection globally

29. Implement Mapbox's Vision SDK for AR-enhanced location services

30. Use OpenCorporates API to track new business registrations in emerging sectors
