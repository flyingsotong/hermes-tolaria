# What I want for myself in 2025

_I find deep satisfaction whenever I can help people and businesses achieve their goals. I'm good at seeing patterns, opportunities and challenges, and creating different ways to proceed. Learning excites me and I'm always looking for new ways to do that. These give me purpose._ (via Clifton Strengths Personality Report)


-**Health, mobility, energy.** Show up. Go from zero to one. Hit the gym. Get back on the bike.

-**Reboot my coaching practice.** Finish the ACC credentialing by mid-year.

-**Get a board seat** so I'm learning something new and contributing in new ways.

-**Decrease my involvement with Splice.** Start something for myself, or take on new work, maybe as a media lecturer.

-**Learn how to facilitate a workshop.** Get certified for this.

-**Be an expert on helping people with career transitions.** Learn new frameworks on career changes.

-**Get my PPL.** Build a community around early PPL students.
