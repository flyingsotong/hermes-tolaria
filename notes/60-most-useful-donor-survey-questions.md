# 60 Most Useful Donor Survey Questions

**Why This Resource Exists**

This resource exists to help fundraisers gain deeper insights into the motivations, preferences, and experiences of their donors across different giving levels—annual, mid- level, and major gifts. By asking the right questions, organizations can better understand how to engage, recognize, and build lasting relationships with their donors, ultimately enhancing donor satisfaction and increasing support for their mission. This curated set of survey questions provides a streamlined approach to gathering valuable feedback and strengthening donor relationships.

**How To Use This Resource**

To use this resource, select the donor segment you wish to survey—annual giving, mid-level giving, or major donors—and choose the most relevant questions from each category.

Customize the questions to fit your organization’s specific goals, whether you want to improve donor engagement, understand motivations, or explore opportunities for increased giving.

Use the questions in online surveys, personal conversations, or follow-up emails to gather valuable insights, then apply the feedback to tailor your donor stewardship strategies and enhance fundraising efforts.

**Annual Giving Questions**

What inspired you to support our organization this year?

How did you first learn about our organization?

On a scale of 1-5, how satisfied are you with the communication you receive from us?

Do you feel your contributions make a difference in achieving our mission?

How would you rate the transparency of our reporting on the impact of donations?

How often do you prefer to receive updates about our work?

What types of updates do you find most engaging?

**What motivates you to continue giving?**

Would you recommend supporting our organization to others?

What could we do to improve your giving experience?

What would encourage you to continue your giving support?

How easy do you find the process of making a donation on our website?

Would you be interested in setting up a recurring gift?

How likely are you to participate in a volunteer opportunity with us?

How do you prefer we acknowledge your donations?

How satisfied are you with the overall donor experience?

How important is it for you to receive updates on how your donation was used?

Would you like to know more about specific ways your donations are making a difference?

What causes within our organization matter most to you?

Would you be interested to support us in other ways besides donations?

**Mid-Level Giving Questions**

What motivates you to make gifts to our organization?

What areas of our work interest you most?

How frequently would you like to receive updates about our work?

On a scale of 1-5, how valued do you feel as a donor?

Do you prefer recognition for your gifts?

How do you prefer we recognize your contributions?

What would encourage you to increase your giving?

Which communication channels do you prefer for updates?

What improvements could we make to strengthen our relationship with our donor community?

How likely are you to continue giving at the current level in the coming year?

What is the most important factor when deciding to make a gift?

Would you be open to meeting with someone from our team to discuss future giving opportunities?

How likely are you to increase your gift in the next year?

What specific impact would you like to see your gift make?

Do you feel you have enough opportunities to engage with our organization beyond giving?

Would you be interested in learning more about planned giving or legacy gifts?

Which of the following would make your giving experience better?

What challenges, if any, do you face in giving currently?

Would you like to participate in our upcoming donor-only event?

How satisfied are you with the level of recognition you receive as part of your donor experience?

**Major Giving Questions**

What motivates you to make a gift to our organization?

Which area of our mission most appeals to you?

How would you rate the level of involvement and engagement offered to as part of your donor experience?

How do you prefer to be updated on the impact of your gift?

Do you feel that your gift has made a lasting impact on our organization’s goals?

How important is it for you to have a personal relationship with the leadership team?

Would you like to be involved in decision-making for future major initiatives?

How often do you prefer updates on how your gift is being used?

What would make you consider increasing your gift in the future?

What could we do better to engage you as a donor?

What is your preferred method of communication with our organization?

Do you feel well-informed about the long-term strategy of our organization?

What would increase your confidence in making additional major gifts?

Would you consider making a legacy or planned gift to our organization?

How satisfied are you with the impact reports provided on your gift?

Would you be interested in participating in strategic planning sessions?

Which of the following would make you more likely to increase your major gift?

Do you feel your gift has a lasting and sustainable impact on our work?

How likely are you to introduce our organization to your network for potential major gifts?

What factors do you consider when deciding to maintain or increase your major gifts?
