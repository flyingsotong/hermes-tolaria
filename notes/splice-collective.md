# Splice Collective

They want:

- Someone to take care of marketing

- Someone to take them into other formats

- Someone to work through their branding

Splice will hire:

- Producer to take content into other formats (eg. newsletter to IG) $60K

- Digital marketer to handle paid and organic discovery across Google and social $60K

- Branding producer to build distinction and affinity $60K

A 3-year plan:

- Splice pays creator a viable monthly salary for 3 years

- Splice takes a 30% cut of subscriptions, sponsorships, events (after costs)

Splice needs to raise:

- $60K x 3 persons x 3 years for above roles = $540K

- Aim to pay creators a salary of $5K a month x 10 creators = $50K a month x 36 months = $1.8M

- Salaries for 2 Splice co-founders at $10K a month x 36 months x 2 co-founders = $720K

- $150K for other operational costs (eg. software)

Total for 3 years: $3.2M 60% from grants 40% from investors

Requirements

- Creator must be someone we believe can make $50K in the first year

- Willing to let us do what we must with branding and workflows

Variation 1

- Year 1: Splice pays creator 100% salary, takes 0% revenue, takes x% equity stake

- Year 2: Splice pays creator 50% salary, takes 30% revenue generated after costs

- Year 3: Splice pays creator 0% salary, takes 30% revenue generated after costs
