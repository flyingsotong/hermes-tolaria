# Coaching - Amanda Oon

**March 25, 2025**

Amanda approached this coaching conversation with professional and personal inquiries centered around navigating professional uncertainty. Her core objectives:

**Professional landscape understanding:**
- Comprehending the transformative dynamics of the media and journalism industry
- Identifying emerging trends and potential future challenges
- Seeking insights into how technological shifts, particularly AI, might reshape her professional landscape

**Personal career strategy:**
- Exploring skill development strategies to remain professionally relevant
- Understanding how to adapt her current skill set to evolving industry requirements
- Preparing for potential career transitions, especially considering her upcoming personal life change (pregnancy)

**Future preparedness:**
- Gaining perspective on potential career pathways
- Developing a proactive approach to professional development
- Understanding how emerging technologies might impact her career trajectory

**Key insights gained:**

Technological integration: Amanda learned that technological adaptation is not about replacement, but collaboration. AI and emerging technologies are tools to augment human capabilities, not eliminate them — viewing AI as a collaborative partner rather than a competitive threat.

Professional adaptability: Professional relevance will increasingly depend on one's ability to identify personal and professional skill gaps, leverage technological tools to address those gaps, develop a continuous learning mindset, and cultivate skills in prompt engineering and AI collaboration.

Practical next steps:
- Experiment with AI tools like Claude
- Use AI for personal and professional planning
- Develop a habit of technological exploration

**Areas for improvement:**

- Implement a more formalized goal-setting framework
- Create a structured action plan with specific, measurable milestones
- Allocate more time to understanding Amanda's individual aspirations
- Explore her specific concerns and emotional landscape surrounding professional changes
- Provide more granular recommendations for skill acquisition

**What worked:**

- Engaged, dynamic conversational approach
- Used relatable metaphors and personal examples
- Encouraged critical and forward-thinking perspectives
- Demystified technological integration

Recommended follow-up: schedule a follow-up session in 3-6 months; develop a personal technological integration journal; create a skill development checklist; establish periodic professional reassessment checkpoints.
