Anja Herberth, a one-person startup founder of a smart buildings platform, sought guidance on how to efficiently utilize her existing content and resources to reach a wider audience.

Key Learnings:

**Capturing Conversations and Interviews**

- Anja recognized the importance of recording her interviews and conversations, which could then be transcribed and repurposed into various formats.

- This would allow her to extract maximum value from each interaction, rather than relying solely on handwritten notes.

**Leveraging AI-Powered Tools**

- Anja learned about tools like ChatGPT, Claude, and voice-to-text applications that could help her automate and streamline content creation tasks.

- These tools could assist in extracting insights from her white papers, generating new content ideas, and even creating audio versions of her written material.

**Repurposing Content in Multiple Formats**

- Anja understood the potential of transforming her existing text-based content into other formats, such as podcasts, data visualizations, and social media-friendly posts.

- This would enable her to reach a wider audience and cater to different preferences, without having to start from scratch for each new platform.

**Experimenting and Iterating**

- Anja was encouraged to set aside time each week to experiment with these new tools and techniques, gradually refining her processes and finding the best fit for her needs.

- The goal was to empower Anja to become more efficient and leverage her limited resources as a one-person startup.

By adopting these strategies, Anja aimed to amplify the impact of her content, reach a broader audience, and streamline her content creation workflow, ultimately supporting the growth and visibility of her smart buildings platform.
