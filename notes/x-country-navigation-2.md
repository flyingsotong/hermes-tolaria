# RAA X-Country Navigation 2 (RAAus Practice Exam)

**Source:** Pilot Practice Exams - RAAus X-Country Endorsement
**URL:** https://www.pilotpracticeexams.com/exams/raa/raa-cross-country-navigation-2/
**Type:** Multiple choice, 20 questions
**License:** Free practice exam

---

1. **At what height should you change from Unicom or Multicom to Area Frequency?** 1500 ft / 3500 ft / **5000 ft** ✓ / 4500 ft

2. **Where should you first go to find information on planning for flights through military airspace?** **ERSA** ✓ / ADFA / CASA / RAF

3. **Which of the following would you make a MAYDAY call for?** You are lost but have plenty of fuel / The aircraft elevator is jammed and you are controlling pitch with throttle / **Your passenger is short of breath, has severe pain in the shoulder and chest** ✓ / Your radio has failed

4. **What code should you squawk if your radio fails?** 1200 / 1400 / 7700 / **7600** ✓

5. **In addition to Final Reserve Fuel and contingency fuel you are required to carry enough fuel for:** none of these / **a diversion and weather (INTER & TEMPO)** ✓ / a diversion / weather only

6. **On a cross country flight your fixed fuel reserve should be:** 10% / 20 min / **45 min** ✓ / 30 min

7. **When is the latest you are recommended to land during a cross country flight?** **10 min before last light** ✓ / 10 min after sunset / At last light / At sunset

8. **If an RAA aircraft with a fuel burn of 20 L/hr departs for a flight with no alternative airport, what is the departure fuel required?** (200 nm, 10 kts headwind, 110 kt cruise, CAVOK): 61 L / 54 L / 40 L / **46 L** ✓

9. **If weather at destination says INTER, minimum extra fuel?** 15% / 15 min / 30 min / **10%** ✓

10. **Which would trigger a SAR response?** SARTIME expiration / Any scheduled report failure / Incidental information / **Any of these** ✓

11. **Which category of military airspace can you plan a flight through?** RA3 / **RA1** ✓ / RA2 / RA4

12. **When does an RAA aircraft need to submit a flight plan?** **When they intend to use flight following (flight watch)** ✓ / >50 nm from departure / VFR in Class E / Any water crossing

13. **What distance from your departure airport may you fly across land without requiring an ELB?** 25 nm / **50 nm** ✓ / 150 nm / 100 nm

14. **When flying in valleys near high mountains it is best to fly:** any of these / in the middle of the valley / **closest to the upwind mountain** ✓ / closest to the downwind mountain

15. **What code should you squawk if you are in an emergency?** 7500 / 7600 / 1400 / **7700** ✓

16. **On a cross country flight in a small piston aeroplane, your contingency fuel must be:** none of these / **10%** ✓ / 15% / 5%

17. **If weather at your destination has TEMPO weather you are required to carry extra ___ min fuel.** 45 / 15 / 60 / **30** ✓

18. **What code do you squawk in general flight?** **1200** ✓ / 1500 / 1400 / 1000

19. **When flying over water an RAA pilot must wear a life jacket:** if they cannot swim to land / only if they choose / >3 nm from land / **whenever you cannot glide to a suitable landing area** ✓

20. **Which would you use a PAN PAN PAN call for?** **Your passenger is feeling sick but will be ok once on the ground** ✓ / Spotting crashed aircraft / Radio stops working / Bird strike, engine stopped
