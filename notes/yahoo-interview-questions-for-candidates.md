# Yahoo! Interview questions for candidates

Created March 29, 2009
**1. Values**
- What values are most important to you in a news organization?
- How would you describe our mission?
**2. Judgment **
- What makes news in your market?
- What are the key components of a great news story on Yahoo?
- What is the toughest challenge of being a journalist in your market?
- What would get a story into a "most emailed" list?
- What would entice a blogger post on a story?
- What are the best kinds of stories that would be discussed in offices and homes in your market?
- What are your competitors like?
- Name 3 people you most want to interview.
- Name 3 stories that you think aren't getting enough coverage.
- Name 3 stories you want to see covered on Yahoo!
- What are the differences between writing for the Web and writing for print (or the last place you worked)?
**3. Personality**
- What feedback do you normally receive from your bosses. What are your strengths and weaknesses?
- What would you seek to achieve 3 months into the job?
- How do you like to be managed?
- What are you most obsessed about?
- What motivates you?
- What are your daily habits when it comes to news consumption?
**Evaluating candidates**
- Integrity
- Intelligence
- Maturity
**Core values**
Energy: positivism Energize others: galvanize others  Edge: courage on tough decisions Execution: getting the job done Passion: authentic excitement
