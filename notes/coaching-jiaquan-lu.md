**April 11, 2024**

Key points discussed in this coaching call:

1. Jiaquan's current venture "Linking Local" leverages the LinkedIn brand to organize professional networking events in Singapore. He is unsure about the long-term sustainability of this model as it is heavily dependent on the LinkedIn brand.
2. Alan provided insights on how Jiaquan could create a more unique and memorable event experience that goes beyond just networking. He emphasized the importance of focusing on the "hospitality" aspect — creating an event that makes attendees feel special, valued, and emotionally connected.
3. Alan shared examples of his own event "Splice Beta" and how they create a sense of community and belonging for their attendees, even before the event starts, through personalized outreach and curation.
4. The key takeaway was that to create a successful and sustainable event business, Jiaquan needs to focus on designing an experience that delivers true value and emotional connection for the attendees, rather than just relying on the LinkedIn brand.

**Insights**

1. The importance of moving beyond just "networking" and creating an event experience that is memorable, meaningful and emotionally resonant for the attendees.
2. The need to deeply understand the pain points, anxieties and aspirations of the target audience, then designing the event to address those needs.
3. The value of personalization and curation — taking the time to understand each attendee's background, interests and needs, and tailoring the experience accordingly.
4. The power of creating a sense of community and belonging, where attendees feel they are part of something special and exclusive.
5. The potential for events to become a core revenue driver for the business, rather than just a side project, if executed well.

Overall, the coaching call provided Jiaquan with a new framework to think about his event business — shifting the focus from just leveraging the LinkedIn brand to crafting a truly memorable and valuable experience for the attendees. This could unlock new possibilities for the long-term sustainability and profitability of his venture.
