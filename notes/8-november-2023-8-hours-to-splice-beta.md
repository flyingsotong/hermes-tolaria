8 November 2023: 8 hours to [Splice Beta.md](8%20November%202023%208%20hours%20to%20Splice%20Beta/Splice%20Beta.md)

In less than 8 hours from now, Rishad and I will be welcoming some 280 participants from 56 countries to the fourth Splice Beta, our little festival to celebrate the media startup community that we love.

So many people have been trickling into Chiang Mai over the past two days for partner events and it's just incredible that we have this opportunity to see so many wonderful friends again.

This year's stress is a little easier to manage. Apart from last minute changes to the program, the speakers, and participants, it seems like everything that needed to be done has been done. We'll find out in a few hours what we've forgotten... or messed up.

But before that, plenty more hugs, handshakes, and fist bumps. We're lucky to be here.
