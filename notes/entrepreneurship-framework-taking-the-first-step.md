# Entrepreneurship Framework Taking the First Step

**Overcoming Fear & Stigma**

-**Fear of failure:** Often linked to what others might think if the first venture doesn't succeed.

-**Defining success:** As an entrepreneur, it's crucial to define your own metrics for success and failure.

**The "3-Month Experiment" Concept**

-**Permission to experiment:** Give yourself the freedom to try an idea for a limited time.

-**Process:**

 1. Build something.

 2. Launch it.

 3. Validate if there's a market/demand.

 4. If not, shut it down without guilt.

-**Benefits:** Allows for quick validation and reduces the pressure of long-term commitment.

**Framework Requirements**

-**Low cost:** Minimize financial investment.

-**Energy investment:** Requires personal effort.

-**Quick validation:** Aim to determine viability within three months.

**Goal**

- Create a system that enables entrepreneurs to quickly test ideas and determine if a venture aligns with their passion and goals.
