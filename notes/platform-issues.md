# Platform issues

- URL: [https://www.therebooting.com/platform-issues/](https://www.therebooting.com/platform-issues/?ref=the-rebooting-newsletter)

- Description: Building a business around a platform or a platform around a business

- Brian Morrissey

- Highlights

 - For an independent publisher – I try to avoid using “creator” because it’s associated with a certain type of publisher –**the fork in the road is whether to build a business on a platform or a platform around a business.** For most, the answer is a no-brainer. A platform that can abstract all the needed functions of media – creation, distribution, monetization – is worth the tradeoff of loss of control, hewing to the platform’s requirements and even preferred business model.
