Mini Retirements

Society teaches us to:

•Have a job •Get into debt •Retire after 40 years

Instead,

Create media content such as:

•Blogs •Podcasts •Newsletters

Don't defer your dreams until late retirement.

Create work around your lifestyle.

The New Rich:

**"Those who abandon the deferred-life plan and create luxury lifestyles in the present using the currency of the New Rich: time and mobility.” - TF**

Old Rich: •Bound by schedule •Work for themselves

New Rich •Work when they want •People work for them

Fear Setting

Ferris uses this practice every month.

Deconstruct the upsides and downsides of a decision.

What are the costs of action?

What are the costs of inaction?

Create shields to protect the downsides of your decisions.

Instead of paying for the degree, allocate the money towards:

Tactics to deal with Haters:

•Keep calm & carry on. •Living well is the best revenge. •Don't try to get everyone to like you. •10% of people take what you say personally. •If your work is practical, a lot of feedback will be negative.

Keep a jar of awesome:

The Jar of Awesome:

If you're good at achievement and not appreciation,

Do this:

Get a jar & label it "awesome"

Anything exciting that happens that day write it down and put it in the jar.

You won't remember these awesome things, but the notes will remind you on bad days.

**Life is not defined by the answers you receive but by the questions you ask.**

Test the impossible with these questions:

•What if I did the opposite for 48 hours? •What would I do/have/be if I had $10 million? •What do I spend a silly amount of money on?

•What’s the least crowded channel?

•Do I need to make it back the way I lost it?

•What are the worst things that could happen? Could I get back here?

•If I could only work 2 hours per week on my business, what would I do?
