The Marc Lourdes Fellowship supports the careers of young under-represented journalists and media professionals in Asia by covering their travel costs to attend international journalism conferences.
We believe that conferences can provide powerful opportunities to meet new people, learn about new trends, and build relationships with potential mentors and collaborators.
We’re looking for applicants from marginalized communities with the potential to make a significant impact in journalism but lack the necessary financial resources.
The Fellowship is administered by Darsh Kanda and supported by some of his closest friends who were fortunate to have accompanied Malaysian journalist Marc Lourdes in his career until his life was shortened in a road crash in 2022.

For our first round, this Fellowship will support the travel costs of one Malaysian journalist to attend the Global Investigative Journalism Conference in Sweden.
Please send a short cover letter detailing your career, the opportunities you’re looking to create at the conference, and how that would help your work in journalism. September 1 2023 is the deadline for applications.
The Marc Lourdes Fellowship is inspired by the EJN Scholarships for Journalists to Cover Biodiversity Conferences. The EJN Scholarships provide financial assistance to journalists from developing countries to attend international conferences on biodiversity.
