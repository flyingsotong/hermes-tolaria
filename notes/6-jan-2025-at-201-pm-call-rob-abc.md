# 6 Jan 2025 at 201 PM call Rob, ABC

6 Jan 2025 at 2:01 PM call: Rob, ABC

Call with Rob at ABC ID — first business call of the year.


Rob is working on a new media development fund that covers three regions: Pacific (70% of funding), Southeast Asia (20%), and South Asia (10%). The fund will run for at least 2.5 years, with a possible 3-year extension.

Key aspects of what Rob wants to accomplish:

1. The fund aims to strengthen media organizations, but rather than being a direct implementer, they will act as a "managing contractor" working with other organizations to deliver activities.

2. They are currently in the design phase, developing:

 - A theory of change

 - Measurement frameworks

 - Governance structure

 - Resource allocation plans

3. Implementation is expected to begin around February/March, providing financial support for:

 - Infrastructure

 - Equipment

 - Training

 - Other needs as identified

4. Rob has already identified some key challenges from preliminary discussions with media organizations:

 - Sustainability issues (surviving in a complex digital world)

 - Declining advertising revenue

 - Staff retention problems due to compensation constraints

 - Need for better digital content packaging

 - Rising operational costs

5. Rob recognizes they can't solve everything and is looking to:

 - Identify the range of problems

 - Focus on specific areas where they can make the most impact given their resources and timeframe

 - Find innovative approaches rather than just maintaining existing structures

 - Ensure responsible and flexible use of DFAT (Department of Foreign Affairs and Trade) funding

Throughout the conversation, Rob shows openness to Alan's suggestions about working with content creators and influencers rather than traditional media, acknowledging that traditional approaches to media development funding haven't been as effective as hoped.
