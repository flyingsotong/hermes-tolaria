# Basic contract law

1. Capacity: Does the person have the authority to make this promise?

2. Offer: Manifestation of a willingness to make a deal

3. Acceptance: Unconditional willingness to be bound by the offer (mirrors offer)

4. Consideration: Something of value given in exchange for the promise

5. Mutual agreement: Parties need to be on the same page -- meeting of minds

6. Legality: Contract needs to be legal to begin with -- 1) purpose 2) procurement 3) execution

7. Proper form: Writing is required for certain deals, eg. real property
