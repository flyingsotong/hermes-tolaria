# SCARF model

**What are the things that can lead to stress... or resentment? (Alternatively, how do you turn these into positive attributes?)**

1. Status: How do others see me?

2. Certainty: How likely will this happen, or not?

3. Autonomy: Do I have control of this?

4. Relatedness: Am I part of the "in" group or outside of it?

5. Fairness: Am I treated fairly as others?

**What happens to your body?**

1. Heart rate up

2. Blood pressure up

3. Breathing up

4. Alertness up

5. Immunity down

6. Pain effects down

**Manifests itself in**

1. How your body feels

2. Bad habits -- drinking, not taking care four self

3. Triggers fights with others

**Can you identify it?**

1. Sad

2. Shame

3. Disgust

4. Anger

5. Fear

6. Surprise

7. Joy

8. Love

9. Trust

**What can you do about it?**

1. Normalize, embrace, accept

2. Change your interpretation of the event

3. Find positive meaning

4. Be present and understand what's happening

**How can you recover?**

1. Sleep

2. Exercise

3. Food

4. Sunshine

5. Positive mindset
