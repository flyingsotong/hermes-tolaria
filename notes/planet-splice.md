Friends,

We're shutting down Planet Splice. We started it in 2021 to address a need that we heard from you -- create a space where you could meet and share your ideas in this community of media professionals.

We got a nice bump at the start with plenty of signups -- about 300, which isn't too bad!

Unfortunately, the early buzz didn't translate to engagement. People were wonderful in introducing themselves and the work they do -- but that didn't lead to people sharing and having conversations.

It's hard growing a social media network from scratch. New habits are hard to create. And if you didn't download Planet on your phone, or have it bookmarked, it wouldn't have found its way into your life.

And it didn't help that the interface was buggy and awkward. We even tried running a little workshop in there (how to build a newsletter), and that was tough because it just wasn't the right experience.

So we'll shut this version down -- but we still believe there's a need for a way for all of us to gather. We'll revive Planet on another platform.

Where should it live?

a. Telegram (where we already are -- but lacks proper community features) b. Slack (some of you are already familiar with this, but it's not free) c. Discord (I'm guessing most of you aren't at all familiar with this, but it's free and has great community features)

Or should we drop the ideal altogether? What are your thoughts?
