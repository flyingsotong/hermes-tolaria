# AI Impact Series Launch - Key Takeaways

**Introduction**

- Launch of the AI Impact Series by SGTech AI Skills and Training Committee.

- Acknowledgment of attendees as thinkers and practitioners in AI.

**The Problem Statement: Skills Churn and Uncertainty**

- Rapid technological change leads to evolving business models and work requirements.

- This creates "skills churn" and uncertainty for companies regarding AI investment (over-invest vs. under-invest/FOMO).

- Uncertainty stems from AI's nature as a "general-purpose computational capability" (e.g., data crunching, problem-solving, product generation across diverse fields).

- Difficulty in forecasting the next AI-driven disruption.

**Solution: Collective Agility and SGTech's Initiatives**

- If the future cannot be predicted, the next best thing is to improve collective agility (companies and workforce to apply and adapt).

-**SGTech AI Skills and Training Advisory Clinics:** Reached out to companies navigating AI adoption challenges.

-**Key finding from clinics:** High demand for AI business application skills.

-**AI Impact Series:** Result of AISTC's work, launched in partnership with SUTD (Singapore University of Technology and Design).

- Converts identified skills from clinics into training requirements to bridge the gap for companies.

**Program Impact & Success**

- Since July, 7 program runs with 10 more scheduled for the rest of the year.

- Over 90 companies and 160 professionals (C-suite to executives) have participated.

- Participants identified practical use cases in:

 - Demand forecasting

 - Inventory management

 - Lead generation

 - Customer engagement

- Companies are clearer on relevant AI skills needed for their workforce.

**Testimonials & Partnerships**

-**Rockbell International Software:** A participant enrolled her entire team after finding the course useful.

-**SkillsFuture Queen Bee:** Actively working with SGTech to extend the series to its merchant network.

-**Ecosystem Partners:** Singapore Indian Chamber of Commerce & Industry (SICCI), Singapore Business Federation (SBF) are promoting the program.

**Call to Action & Pricing**

-**Cost:** S$254 per head after SSG subsidy (for SMEs).

-**SkillsFuture Enterprise Credit:** Eligible companies can claim up to 90%, reducing net cash payment to S$25.40.

- Encouragement for more companies to join to strengthen teams and embark on AI adoption.

**Moving Beyond the "AI Trough of Disillusionment"**

- Aim to move companies from confusion to a "slope of confidence" (not enlightenment, as technology constantly changes).

- Emphasizes continuous training, adaptation, and transformation due to the constant pace of technological change.
