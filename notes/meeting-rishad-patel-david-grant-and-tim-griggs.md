- 9:00pm met with [Robin Kwong.md](Thu,%20April%204th,%202024/Robin%20Kwong.md)

 - This was an interesting conversation because I wasn't expecting a call like this. Robin was in good spirits, cheerful, and happy. More than anything, he was eager to talk about his feelings regarding his work at the Wall Street Journal, where he's been promoted and given a larger team. He's now the director of audience loyalty or something similar. He's at a point where he is getting the titles he wants, but the runway ahead of him is probably shorter than he imagined. His parents are both in Hong Kong, and he is an only child. Both of his parents now have cancer; his mother has lung cancer, and his father has prostate cancer. This leaves him with a set of options and decisions that he needs to make. So, my coaching question to him was, what does he have to do in the next 10 years so that he won't have any regrets? I left that with him. Let's see where that goes.
