# Coaching - Durga Sengupta

**January 15, 2025**

**Key realizations and outcomes**

1. She identified and articulated her core strengths: extremely organized and deadline-oriented, strong linguistic capabilities (Hindi, Urdu, Malayalam, Bengali), experience as both an editor and fact-checker, adaptability across different organizations and evolving journalism landscapes, deep understanding of tech journalism's evolution beyond product reviews
2. She gained clarity about her career direction: confirmed her commitment to journalism despite opportunities in communications, identified fact-checking as a specific area of interest, recognized the value of her editorial background in fact-checking work, acknowledged her need to develop OSINT (open source intelligence) skills
3. She processed some self-doubt: recognized that her 5 months of fact-checking experience, while brief, is valuable; started to see her cross-organizational experience as an asset; began to reframe her perception of her qualifications

---

**Questions that could have been valuable**

Career strategy:

- "What would an ideal mix of freelance work look like for you in terms of fact-checking versus other journalistic work?"
- "How might you leverage your multilingual skills more intentionally in your freelance strategy?"
- "What does success look like for you in 6 months? In 1 year?"

Professional development:

- "What specific aspects of OSINT would be most valuable for your career goals?"
- "How might you create a structured learning path for yourself while freelancing?"
- "What networks or professional communities could support your growth in fact-checking?"

Personal wellbeing:

- "How can you structure your freelance work to accommodate your health needs?"
- "What boundaries do you need to set to maintain work-life balance as a freelancer?"
- "What support systems do you need to put in place for this transition?"

Market positioning:

- "How might you position your unique combination of editorial and fact-checking experience?"
- "What kinds of stories or projects would best showcase your cross-cultural understanding?"
- "How could your experience in both legacy and digital media be an advantage?"

The conversation helped her move from a place of uncertainty to beginning to see her unique value proposition, though there's room for more specific strategy development and action planning.
