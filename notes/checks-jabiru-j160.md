**Takeoff**

- Push nose down slightly for 70 to avoid stall

- Pull brake to stop wheel spin

- Nut to the wall (RPM down to protect engine)

- At 400 ft — call safe alt, flaps up, fuel pump off (radio interference)

- Turning downwind radio call

**Downwind**

1. 2,900 rpm

2. Trim set

3. Downwind Checks

- Ts & Ps GREEN

* Carb Heat ON, Light ON, Fuel Pump ON

- Brakes — Pressured and OFF

- Hatches & Harnesses — Secure

- Enough fuel to go around

**Final**

1. Aim point

2. Wind sock

3. Airspeed at 70

4. Carby off

5. Fuel pump on


**Speeds (KIAS)**

- Initial Climb 1 stage = 70

- Cruise Climb = 80-85

- Base = 70-75

- Final = 65-70

- Glide = 70


**On runway**

- Turn yoke into wind — if in front and on the sides

- Turn yoke away from the wind — if it’s coming from behind
