# Beta 2024 Travel logistics

Plenty of moving parts at this moment. But let's get you to Beta!

Here's the fun part -- we don’t have a dedicated travel agent, so we’ll need you to handle your own flight and hotel bookings. But it’ll also make it easier for you to figure out when you’re flying in and out, make amendments, and it will also give you some flexibility if you have a preference for different rooms, or if you're traveling with family.

**1. FLIGHTS**

I looked at prices on Google Travel. A return flight from Manila to Chiang Mai should be around US$300 right now.

If your flight is +/- 10% of that number, please go ahead and book. If it’s more than that amount, please let me know before booking. But book early in the next couple of weeks before flight prices go up!

Beta runs on Nov 5-7.

On Nov 5, we're running a full day of clinics, followed by the opening dinner that evening. If you don't want to attend the clinics, you can just fly in on Nov 5 if you like. If you're planning on attending the clinics, fly in on Nov 4 instead.

And then it’s two full days of festivities on November 6-7. You can then jump back on the plane on November 8 if you’re in a hurry.
