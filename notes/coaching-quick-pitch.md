I coach journalists and media entrepreneurs across their careers. My goal is to help provide insight and awareness around change — either building a media startup, or transitioning to a different role in the industry.

"I'm afraid I'll fail." "I'm worried that I won't find customers." "I feel like I don't know enough to be doing this." "I think my career has stalled."

We focus on areas that are holding them back in this process — their fears and beliefs.
