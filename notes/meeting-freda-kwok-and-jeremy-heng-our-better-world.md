# Meeting Freda Kwok and Jeremy Heng – Our Better World

Wed, July 31st, 2024

- [Links](Links.md)

 - Nike: An Epic Saga of Value Destruction (Massimo Giunco)

- 2:00pm met with Freda Kwok, Jeremy Heng

Good meeting with Our Better World. Their need is to figure out how to better articulate what they do and how they do it to their funders and to partners. It's a 12-year-old team that's been running semi-independently of the Singapore International Foundation. They even sit in separate locations from SIF.

But there is a new leadership in place with new chairman and CEO... and so now they have to justify their work.

They've been running as a production company — working with producers and filmmakers across SEA. "Storytelling" came up a lot.

They don't know what's the impact of that storytelling (mostly inspirational videos of people/NGOs "doing good").

SIF's goal is to build cultural ties with the region — public diplomacy, hearts and minds. So OBW needs to align with some of that.

Jeremy sees a lot of parallels with Splice — how we, as a small team, have been able to position ourselves as relationship/community builders, hero-making, etc.

Possible deliverables for their $20K that came up in the convo:\
1\. Help us understand OBW's value to our funders and partners — what are the needs we're addressing? What is the role of our storytelling in that process — or if it's even useful.\
2\. Help us turn OBW into a convening hub where we can add value to the work of NGOs in the region. We want to showcase important work (eg. climate, women, environment) in the region, and bring Singapore's expertise into it.\
3\. Help us position OBW as part of Singapore's efforts in the information integrity space — how can we take Singapore's work in that space to other countries?
