**About the Inbox Collective + Not a Newsletter audience**

- About Inbox Collective

- The website averages about 4,000 unique visitors and 7,000 total visits per month.

- Readers average about 3 minutes per visit.

- About Not a Newsletter

- More than 9,000 readers subscribe to the Not a Newsletter email list.

- The email list has averaged exactly a 50% open rate over the previous year, virtually unchanged since the roll out of Apple’s Mail Privacy Protection update. The click-to-open rate for the list is 32.9%.

- About the entire audience

- These properties are most read in the U.S., U.K., Germany, Denmark, and Canada, though it also has notable readerships elsewhere, including in Australia, Brazil, France, and South Africa.

- Readers typically fall into one of four categories:

- People who are directly involved with email at a news organization, like a newsletter editor or director of audience development.

- People who lead email strategy for a non-profit or NGO.

- People who work at an email technology or strategy company.

- People who have created an independent newsletter.

- According to surveys of these readers:

- 84% say they read it for ideas or inspiration to implement in their email strategy.

- 80% say they use it to find tools and resources to apply to their newsletter.

- 79% say they read it to identify email best practices.

- 93% say Not a Newsletter is a useful resource for them.
