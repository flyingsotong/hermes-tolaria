 - “A creator, in the internet sense, is somebody who makes something for the internet, whether for their own accounts or for other accounts.

Whenever I use the word ‘creator,’ I'm sort of using it up against 'influencer.’

An influencer is somebody who's selling their _lifestyle_ on the internet. Whereas a creator is somebody who's like selling their _craft_ on the internet.”

Rachel Karten, creator of _Link in Bio_, [in _Creator Spotlight_](https://www.creatorspotlight.com/p/rachel-karten-link-in-bio)
