**August 23, 2023**

- Started by talking about imposter syndrome
- We took that label off and she gave it another name — doubt
- We talked about how that doubt manifests — what's the language? What's the body language? What's the change in her relationships?
- Has moved from job to job seeking affirmation — things that she doesn't have in her family
- Mom had depression and she took it on herself to be the rescuer
- Work is now a proxy for that — she wants to have "harmony" at work
- In building unreasonable expectations of herself, she's undermining herself by creating doubt
- Her insight: I'm letting my doubt undermine my workspace, and I now know when I'm telling myself things that eventually sabotage me
- My insight: I'm proud of how I was able to connect those dots, check in with her, ask permission to proceed in a course of thinking
