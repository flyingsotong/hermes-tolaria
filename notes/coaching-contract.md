**What coaching is**

Building rapport, setting goals, exploring options for sustainable change, challenging new thinking in the context of support, reflecting on new understandings in terms of desired organisational outcomes, gaining commitment for actions, providing support for sustainability of learning and actions.

**What it isn't**

Coaching is NOT counselling, mentoring, leading, managing, consulting or training.

**Top-level outcomes you can expect**
- Your career goals and a clear plan of where your career is going
- Identify your strengths and where you find meaning
- Build your confidence as you manage change and uncertainty
- Identify your brand
- Learn how to articulate and sell yourself

**Confidentiality**

Any specific information discussed, or issues that are raised, during coaching will be on a strictly confidential basis and will not be communicated in any way with persons outside the coaching relationship. Alan will maintain notes on the progress of the coaching sessions and will ensure complete confidentiality of this information. Alan will work within the professional ethics guidelines designated by the International Coaching Federation. Copies of the ethical guidelines are available on request. A Coaching Contract will be signed at the commencement of the Coaching Program.
