# 8 years of Splice

2023 will be remembered as the year of massive layoffs in tech. Eight years ago, I too had lost my job as part of a plan to pull back on investments and staff.

Yahoo's retreat from Southeast Asia gave me one of the best opportunities in life. At 41, I was neither young nor old, but it was definitely a moment where big -- and wrong -- financial decisions would no longer be reversible.

The Splice Newsroom, the precursor to what we are today as Splice Media, was registered on February 19, 2015. It was Chinese New Year and, as we do every year, we spent the night at my parents'. The dining table was where I registered the company, and in 15 minutes, it was done.

It might still be too soon to call success at eight years. But here we are with purpose and profit, and a community to call our own. Friendships, luck, and hard work made all the difference.

If you've lost your job recently, take heart -- you have more opportunities today than I did eight years ago.

The barrier to entry is lower than ever because of the internet. You now have access to people from all around the world to count on, and thanks to the lessons from the pandemic, everyone is just a Zoom call away. You can work and live practically anywhere.

Starting something may be the best thing you've done for yourself. Reach out if you think I can help.
