**SCMP Newsroom Optimization Discussion**

**Background**

- South China Morning Post (SCMP) is undergoing transformation, focusing on editorial changes
- Previous transformation (6-8 years ago) focused on becoming mobile-enabled
- Current focus is on improving productivity and efficiency

- Current Situation

 - Newsroom has over 200 people, producing about 100 articles daily

 - Cost is very high, with 2.5 to 3 people working on one article per day

 - SCMP has multiple desks: Hong Kong, China, Business, Technology, Culture, Opinion, etc.

 - Recent staff turnover due to COVID-19 and other factors

- Optimization Goals

 - Improve efficiency and productivity in the newsroom

 - Reduce costs while maintaining quality

 - Optimize workflow and reduce unnecessary steps

 - Potentially reduce output (number of articles) while maintaining reader satisfaction

- Challenges

 - Editorial team resistance to external input

 - Balancing editorial independence with business needs

 - Complexity of current workflow and CMS

- Proposed Approach

 - Analyze current workflow and identify inefficiencies

 - Consider reducing output from 100 to 50 articles per day

 - Review content categories and their importance

 - Optimize CMS to reduce steps and improve efficiency

 - Examine reader behavior and focus on high-value content

- Next Steps

 - Set up a call between the consultant and Catherine (SCMP CEO)

 - Discuss potential collaboration and project scope

 - Consider timeline and resource availability for the optimization project
