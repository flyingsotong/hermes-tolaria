|**Air:** Carb heat | Turn on carb heat to melt any carb icing if any. Carb icing may cause engine roughness or in some major circumstances, may cause an engine to stop. |
|**Fuel:** 1. Fuel Pump 2. Fuel Selector Switch | When the engine driven fuel pump goes out, it stops the fuel supply into the engine. When the fuel selector is accidentally turned off or a pilot forgot to switch tanks, it might cause the engine to be starved of fuel and eventually causing it to fail. |
|**Spark:** Magnetos | Cycle the magnetos and try to crank the aircraft back to life. |
