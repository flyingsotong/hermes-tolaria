**June 7, 2023**

- Has lost her spirit after 4 years of doing this — the hard path of entrepreneurship but also the political and economic context of Pakistan
- Her mind is tired and unexcited by the work — and yet doesn't want to shut it down
- Her awareness: What do I want the next 6–12 months to look like? What does success look like for me? How do I swim and not just float through? What am I giving up to do something I no longer enjoy?
- My awareness: Need to learn how to ask better questions around success and failure; need to be willing to go deeper — what are these stories they tell themselves? How was she raised? What are you normalizing that shouldn't be? What is the hardest question you're not asking yourself?
